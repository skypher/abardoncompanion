The 32 Paths of Wisdom

Path #1

Sephirah 1: Kether

Gen 1:1 -- "In the beginning Elohim created the heaven and the earth."

Path #2

Elemental 1: Heh

Gen 1:2 -- "Now the earth was unformed and void, and darkness was upon the face of the deep; and the spirit of Elohim hovered over the face of the waters."

Path #3

Sephirah 2: Chokmah

Gen 1:3 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let there be light.' And there was light."

Path #4

Double 1: Beth

Gen 1:4 -- "And Elohim saw the light, that it was good."

Path #5

Elemental 2: Vav

Gen 1:4 -- "and Elohim divided the light from the darkness."

Path #6

Elemental 3: Zayin

Gen 1:5 -- "And Elohim called the light Day, and the darkness He called Night. And there was evening and there was morning, one day."

Path #7

Sephirah 3: Binah

Gen 1:6 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let there be a firmament in the midst of the waters, and let it divide the waters from the waters."

Path #8

Mother 1: Aleph

Gen 1:7 -- "And Elohim made the firmament, and divided the waters which were under the firmament from the waters which were above the firmament; and it was so."

Path #9

Elemental 4: Cheth

Gen 1:8 -- "And Elohim called the firmament heaven. And there was evening and there was morning, a second day."

Path #10

Sephirah 4: Chesed

Gen 1:9 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let the waters under the heaven be gathered together unto one place, and let dry land appear.' And it was so."

Path #11

Elemental 5: Teth

Gen 1:10 -- "And Elohim called the dry land earth, and the gathering together of the waters called He seas."

Path #12

Double 2: Gimel

Gen 1:10 -- "and Elohim saw that it was good."

Path #13

Sephirah 5: Geburah

Gen 1:11 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let the earth put forth grass, herb yielding seed, and fruit-tree bearing fruit after its kind, wherein is the seed thereof, upon the earth.' And it was so."

Path #14

Double 3: Daleth

Gen 1:12 -- "And the earth brought forth grass, herb yielding seed after its kind, and fruit-tree bearing fruit, wherein is the seed thereof, after its kind; and Elohim saw that it was good."

Gen 1:13 -- "And there was evening and there was morning, a third day."

Path #15

Sephirah 6: Tiphareth

Gen 1:14 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let there be lights in the fermament of the heaven to divide the day from the night; and let them be for signs, and for seasons, and for days and years.'"

Gen 1:15 -- "'and let them be for lights in the firmament of the heaven to give light upon the earth.' And it was so."

Path #16

Mother 2: Mem

Gen 1:16 -- "And Elohim made the two great lights: the greater light to rule the day, and the lesser light to rule the night; and [He made] the stars.

Path #17

Elemental 6: Yod

Gen 1:17 -- "And Elohim set them in the firmament of the heaven to give light upon the earth,"

Gen 1:18 -- "and to rule over the day and over the night, and to divide the light from the darkness;"

Path #18

Double 4: Kaph

Gen 1:18 -- "and Elohim saw that it was good."

Gen 1:19 -- "And there was evening and there was morning, a fourth day."

Path #19

Sephirah 7: Netzach

Gen 1:20 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let the waters swarm with swarms of living creatures, and let fowl fly above the earth in the open firmament of heaven.'"

Path #20

Elemental 7: Lamed

Gen 1:21 -- "And Elohim created the great sea-mosters, and every living creature that creepeth, wherewith the waters swarmed, after its kind, and every winged fowl after its kind;"

Path #21

Double 5: Peh

Gen 1:21 -- "and Elohim saw that it was good."

Path #22

Elemental 8: Nun

Gen 1:22 -- "And Elohim blessed them, saying: 'Be fruitful and multiply, and fill the waters in the seas, and let fowl multiply in the earth.'"

Gen 1:23 -- "And there was evening and there was morning, a fifth day."

Path #23

Sephirah 8: Hod

Gen 1:24 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let the earth bring forth the living creature after its kind, cattle, and creeping things, and beast of the earth after its kind.' And it was so."

Path #24

Mother 3: Shin

Gen 1:25 -- "And Elohim made the beast of the earth after its kind, and the cattle after their kind, and every thing that creepeth upon the ground after its kind;"

Path #25

Double 6: Resh

Gen 1:25 -- "and Elohim saw that it was good."

Path #26

Sephirah 9: Yesod

Gen 1:26 -- "And Elohim said: 'Let us make man in our image, after our likeness; and let them have dominion over the fish of the sea, and over the fowl of the air, and over the cattle, and over all the earth, and over every creeping thing that creepeth upon the earth.'"

Path #27

Elemental 9: Samekh

Gen 1:27 -- And Elohim created man in His own image;"

Path #28

Elemental 10: Ayin

Gen 1:27 -- "in the image of Elohim created He him; male and female created He them."

Path #29

Elemental 11: Tzaddi

Gen 1:28 -- "And Elohim blessed them;"

Path #30

Sephirah 10: Malkuth

Gen 1:28 -- "and Elohim said unto them: 'Be fruitful, and multiply, and replenish the earth, and subdue it; and have dominion over the fish of the sea, and over the fowl of the air, and over every living thing that creepeth upon the earth.'"

Path #31

Elemental 12: Qooph

Gen 1:29 -- "And Elohim said: 'Behold, I have given you evey herb yielding seeed, which is upon the face of the earth, and every tree, in which is the fruit of a tree yielding seed -- to you it shall be for food;'"

Gen 1:30 -- "'and to every beast of the earth, and to every fowl of the air, and to every thing that creepeth upon the earth, wherein there is a living soul, [I have given] every green herb for food.' And it was so."

Path #32

Double 7: Tav

Gen 1:31 -- "And Elohim saw everything that He had made, and behold, it was very good. And there was evening and there was morning, the sixth day."
