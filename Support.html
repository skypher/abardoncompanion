Self-Healing Archaeous

Home

Since ABardonCompanion.com was founded 5½ years ago, it has grown and grown and grown . . . far beyond my expectations.  When I started out on this adventure in 2001, I had no idea of the amount of time, effort and, eventually, money this website would demand of me.  Time and effort seem to be in unlimited supply, but for me, money is not.  :) 


For me, the great burden of maintaining this website has been its cost.  What started out as only $5.00 a month has blossomed into $25.00 a month, and that's just for the hosting plan with sufficient bandwidth to keep the site open for the whole month!  If I add in all the incidental expenses such as maintaining an internet access, upgrading programs and computer equipment, etc., then it comes to around $100.00 a month, averaged out over these 5½ years.


I had hoped that sales of my CDs and later, of my art work (through RawnMade.com), would support ABC.com but that has not worked out.  Sales of my CDs have been too slow to make any difference and RawnMade.com has proven a pointless venture which is costing me more to keep open than it has actually netted.  A financial wizard I am not!  ;-) 


At present, in an average month, ABC.com consumes 15 Gigs of bandwidth and serves over 18,000 visitors.   If each visitor were to donate just $.01 (one cent), ABC.com would have no financial problems!  ;-)  Don't worry, I'm not going to turn ABC.com into a membership-only pay site!  ;-)  ;-)  ;-)  But I do need your help in order to keep ABC.com going . . .


Clearly, this website is of value to a lot of people so I am asking that those of you who do find value here, express your appreciation for this resource by making a donation to help support ABC.com's continuation.  As an incentive, I am offering the following packages, each "priced" so that there is ample money left over to help cover website expenses.
