Grins... If you had a Zen master he would whap you up the back of 
the head and say; that didn't happen.

It's something about where your mind is at these certain places in 
the exercises. 

Try this;

Think this, with your eyes closed... 

I am cold,
I am hot,
I am cold,
I am hot,
I am cold,
I am neither.

And sit and feel that "I am neither."

--------------------------
As I mentioned before, the process of chopping down each thought at 
it's origination point actually brings you to a very curious 
place/space. Suddenly, after a successful session of chopping down 
thoughts at their roots you are left with thoughts 'about' to 
present themselves, and you will see 'where' they are coming from 
within you. This may amuse you when you find it. It may set you off 
wondering other things. It is always profitable to find this space.

Or... you could go on endlessly using your mind to construct another 
turn in the never ending labyrinth of intrigue and detail that your 
mind is able to conjure to block you from continuing to do any of 
the actual exercises. Winks. :)

Close your eyes, let everything rise that comes, just as if it were 
heat rising, sooner or later you will see the "I" who is 
orchestrating all of this.

I tend to be an experience type of person, some on this board are 
more the intellectual type and they will probably help you more. For 
me, I only 'sincerely' wish you the best. We need every worker we 
can get.

I wish you well,
Chuck
