I just e-mailed the author of this book (I believe someone asked 
about his book a while back, because it is out of print). This is

Dear David,

I've discussed a reprint of Paths of Wisdom with the acquisitions 
editor at Weiser, and am beginning the process (a somewhat elaborate

2005 at the earliest.

With my best,

John Michael Greer
