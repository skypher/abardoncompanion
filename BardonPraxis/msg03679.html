Hi,

I don't know anything about any specific Hermetic writings or 
techniques on the topic of self-defense, but my personal opinion is 
that part of the living work of Hermetic practice is to continually 
bring practical, day-to-day existence into communion with higher law 
through one's own contemplation and actions. It's the same as with 
healing, where all the levels have to be addressed: physical, 
astral/emotional, and mental. In this analogy of healing, if I 
experience pain, I apply techniques to stop the pain; I open my 
awareness to the emotional experience of the pain; I investigate the 
root of the pain and contemplate what meaning it has and what it has 
to teach me.

It sounds like a very difficult situation you are in and certainly 
it would be a tremendous challenge to anyone's sense of security and 
equilibrium in the world. I don't have such experience with the 
threat of violence, but if I were in that situation, I think I would 
first focus on what things to do to keep me and my loved ones alive 
and safe for the immediate present, and turn to Hermetic practice to 
help me make sense of all that is happening and how to deal with it 
both emotionally and in a long-term sense, e.g. in terms of soul 
mirror work and character transformation.


My two cents, for what it's worth.
David
