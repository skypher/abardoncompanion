your physical body. If you wish for example that your patience

exercise, plus a continuous instrospetion every day, plus water 
magic, plus a conscious developed enough to pay attention in your

during the mental instruction of step 1), and working with one 
problem at a time, you certainly will achieve your objective, sooner 
or later.
