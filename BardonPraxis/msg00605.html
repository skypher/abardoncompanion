>> Is it normal when visualising, that the picture is moving on itself ?
(different view angles). <<

It is fairly "normal", I suppose, for this to occur at first, but
ultimately, you will need to be in control of your visualizations. I
suggest that when a visualization begins to take on a life of its own,
you immediately dismiss it and start over. These are supposed to be
*your* creations and thus they should behave as *you* desire.
