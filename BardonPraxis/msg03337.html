Dear Sarah,

The answer is that you must work all three sections of each Step
(mental, astral and physical) simultaneously. Begin with the first
exercise of each section and as you master each exercise of a section,
move on to the next exercise in that section. The Step is considered
complete when you have worked your way through all the exercises in each
section.

Bardon emphasized a balanced progression and this can only be
accomplished if you work on the mental, astral and physical aspects
simultaneously. :)
