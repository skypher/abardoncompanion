I wonder if there is a medium anywhere, either trance or direct-voice or
automatic-writing, who would be sufficiently capable of channeling Franz
Bardon to enable him to complete "The Golden Book of Wisdom" and his
Alchemy book that way? This sort of thing has been done before: the
second half of the last book of Dion Fortune, "Moon Magic", left
uncompleted by her untimely death in 1946 aged 55, was completed soon
after her death through an automatic-writing medium who was a member of
her Society of Inner Light based in London, England. Other material was
also received from her this way.
