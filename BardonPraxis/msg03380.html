It seems to me, that often these 'ruthless' questions logically thought to be at the beginning of a venture to weigh the cost, so to speak, cannot be trusted. When has anything significant been done so 'conveniently' and perhaps even comfortably? As in many things in life, our 'already-listeing-opinion' is that we can accomplish the task of ascertaining the cost. Actually, we cannot. ANd in my opinion, should simply carry on as best one may and adjust as motion dictates. Intent shall win the day.. even if the day is delayed, metaphorically speaking. :) ty. :T: 

It seems that if there´s ONE quality that is central here, it would be
ruthless honesty: How much do I really want it? How much am I prepared to
pay for it?
