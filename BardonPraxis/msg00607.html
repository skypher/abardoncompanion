Dear Woce,

>> I have question concerning the exercise of visualisation
Step 2. What quality of mental picture must we obtain ? <<

In the "Essays & Correspondence" section of my website,
there are
several excerpts on this subject that you might find
helpful. If they
don't clarify this issue for you, please let me know and
I'll toss a few
more words into the mix. :)

>> Is it normal when visualising, that the picture is
moving on itself ?
(different view angles). <<

It is fairly "normal", I suppose, for this to occur at
first, but
ultimately, you will need to be in control of your
visualizations. I
suggest that when a visualization begins to take on a life
of its own,
you immediately dismiss it and start over. These are
supposed to be
*your* creations and thus they should behave as *you*
desire.

>> For the rest I'll be patient and let my experience do
the job. <<

Good! :) Experience is The Best teacher of all.
