Dear Simon,

>> In your experience, is it best to focus on one soul-mirror-item at a
time? Or does it seem more effective to address any issue that is on
your list as it arises? <<

It is best to *focus* all of your efforts upon one item at a time and
continue that focus until that specific item is resolved to your
satisfaction. However, this doesn't mean you should ignore other Soul
Mirror items when they arise. :)

That moment when you recognize that a Soul Mirror item is arising is a
moment of great power in which you have the option to choose a more
positive alternative behaviour. Just because an issue arises that
you're not focusing all the rest of your efforts upon, doesn't mean that
you should pass up this most valuable opportunity for self-change.

The "one item at a time" rule refers to your application of the various
techniques such as auto-suggestion, magic of food, water and breath,
meditations, etc. These should all be focused upon one item at a time
until its resolution.
