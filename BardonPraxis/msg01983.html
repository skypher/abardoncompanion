Thank you, everyone who responded to my question about emotional 
contagion. What a rich collection of insights and strategies for 
dealing with this (thankfully rare) problem.

The interaction of question and replies has however drawn me into 
curiosity about one aspect of this matter. Though I have looked 
elsewhere, including Web search engines and other occult literature, 
the only author I have found who mentions this idea of larvae is 
Franz Bardon, and he really does not elaborate on it much.

A fascinating thought, really -- a discarnate thoughtform, not a pre-
existing angel, demon, or elemental, but the creation of a human 
being as the result of intense emotion. Further, by his description, 
these annoyances are as likely, or more likely, to be spun off 
inadvertently -- their creators are typically oblivious to having 
done such a thing.

I am curious about two things:

1. Assuming you accept the existence of these things, is there 
more to say about them than Bardon's brief mention?

2. Obviously, their presence does not call for a full-blown 
exorcism, so what might you do if consulted by a non-practitioner for 
psychic or occult assistance, and you determined that this was the 
person's problem?

Warmest regards,
