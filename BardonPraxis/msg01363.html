In "Souvenirs de Franz Bardon" / "Erinnerung an Franz Bardon" (No english
translation), Lumir Bardon (Franz's son), says that his father had a very
normal diet during his life. Only once - before Lumir's birth - did Franz
Bardon follow a strictly vegetarian diet for 40 days, prior to a specific
magical operation. This was told to Lumir by his mother.
