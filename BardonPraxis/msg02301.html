Hi David,

Think of mental exercises in exactly the same way as you think of 
phsyical exercises. If you start from scratch and go to the gym non-
stop for two weeks, you'll end up tired despite the immediate changes 
you can see. However, if you stick the "gym" for two months, what 
tired you at first ceases to tire you and you become stronger and 
fitter - this is what is happening.

In early stages, it is advisable to have breaks ever few days or over 
the weekend to let your mind mellow a little, as you would going to 
the gym!

Hope that helps,

Mark
