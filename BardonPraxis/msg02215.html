Dear emcshadow!

Thank you for that great post! It goes a long way to show the 
significance of impregnation and how all the exercises overlap.

It made me think of a good example of successful impregnation in 
action. Before I mention what it is, let me just say that it proves 
the potency of impregnation of ideas / states of being. It is a great 
verification for the technique to the novice.

What I am thinking of is the way that states of being are imprinted 
into the music we listen to. I am sure that most people have 
experienced this; you hear a song that you haven't heard for a very 
long time, and it conjures up the state of mind you were in when you 
used to listen to it regularly. We call it nostalgic, because we've 
imprinted that particular music with the particular way we were and 
felt back when we listened to it alot.

Just some thoughts that popped into my head... :-)
