Hello Juli,
Bursts of light and sound are common as your skills in meditation progresses, and as for percieving the infinite... well that is a major meditative mile-stone, congrats. The key is to do nothing. Avoid thinking things like "I did it, I did it!" and "Am I doing this right?" and "What should I do?", rather simply be in the experience. Eventually you will be able to do these things with less and less effort and they will be of great help in your magical/mystical practice as well as in (IMO), managing daily life.
