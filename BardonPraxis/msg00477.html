unnecessary. If not, then it would be essential to understand the
theory before attempting the practice.

if you have completed Step Eight. But again, none of this matters much
if you haven't already completed Step Eight or the equivalent since
Bardon's Quabbalah is based upon abilities learned through the IIH work.
