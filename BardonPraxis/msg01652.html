Hello, 
>> Is the so-called "mind chatter" (the internal mental discussion) an
item to be inserted into the soul mirror or is it a pure spirit-related
activity ...since it's an shortcoming corrected at level 1 under
"spirit" ? <<
Rawn replied:
Mind chatter is not a "shortcoming". It's a natural aspect of the human
mind. It's just how the brain functions and is not an emotional defect.
Therefore, it is not appropriate for the negative Soul Mirror.

Rawn has an excellent point. Mind chatter itself, is a function of the human condition... that is why so many eastern systems, as well as everything in the west from Martinism to FBs work includes methods of quieting the "chattering monkey", but certain traits which may include "distractability", "daydreaming", "obsessing about the future", "dwelling on the past", and so on, while all ostensibly could be tied into mind chatter (and may be too general), are not mind chatter itself but habits one has developed that channel the chattering of the mind. Therefore, as habits, they may be acceptable addendums to a negative soul mirror.
