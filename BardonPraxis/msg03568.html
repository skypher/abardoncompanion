In addition to the excellent suggestions so far, this may help. 

It may be useful to think of thought-forms, in this case your
'ideations', as energetic constructs rather than intellectual ones. 

It is difficult for the modern mind to conceptually bridge the
descartian divide and push an intellectual construct into some
material mass no? Part of this exercise is retraining this erroneous
conditioning and realizing the rich energetic connections that abound
in a superficially material world. You may have experienced this
linkage when you think of a friend the same time the phone rings with
their call. Or felt someone looking at you from across the room. Your
thought-forms are energetically interacting with your reality both
within and without constantly. Usually without awareness or intent.

And in addition to this shift of perception of internal thought-forms
as energetic constructs, also break down the eucharist into an
energetic matrix. Trace the food, for example, back to the plant forms
and elemental energies, the sun, water, air and earth that are part of
the timestream of its existence and consider the alchemical processes
at work absorbing this into your body and the way the energy is being
processed, transfered and transmuted during this interaction. Then the
coupling may feel more natural, as indeed it is. When you start
feeling this connection as a fabric rather than an artificial
dichotomy between subject and object, you've started to reprogram your
reality in some very interesting and useful ways.
