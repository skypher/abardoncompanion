Dear Lawrence,

>> I was wondering... If the Karma isn´t stored in the psysical body,
why had Franz Bardon to live with the Karma of the son of Viktor Bardon?
<<

The Individual's Karma is what *causes* the body and the life
circumstances which that body must undergo or experience. While these
are not "stored" in the physical body, the physical body's aliveness
cannot be separated from the causal karma.

The karmic lessons that the Individual is predestined to face during
incarnation must be taken over by the spirit "walking into" the body,
otherwise the "walk in" would incur a very, very heavy karmic debt for
essentially robbing the original spirit of the opportunity to redeem
their karma through the specific circumstances of that incarnation.
