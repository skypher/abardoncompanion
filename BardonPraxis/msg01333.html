Dear Mahazou,

>> I would appreciate if someone could help me to understand a very
curious phenomema happening every time i tried to focus my awarness on
my skin in the exercice of complete awarness of tne astral body.During
this phase, i feel like if i was sucked by a fast whirlwind turning
aroud my physical body. One feel a couple of sensation both in the
physical body and in the whirlwind. Have some one experiment this ? What
its mean ? <<

This is a fairly common initial experience of the astral body that has
to do with the differences between the astral and physical "energies",
for lack of a better word. Compared to the physical, the astral is very
dynamic and this can be disorienting until one adjusts to the
difference.

The key to making this adjustment is to not resist the motions you
sense. Flow *with* them instead of trying to hold on to the physical
body's relative motionlessness. After a short while, these sensations
will diminish.

Another action that will help you to adjust is to continue on with the
Archaeous exercise and work with the awareness of the four Elemental
regions of the astral body, integrating them together and then
re-integrating your astral body into your physical body's awareness.
