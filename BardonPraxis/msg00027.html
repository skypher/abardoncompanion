Dear Larry,

Welcome to BardonPraxis! :)

Thanks for your kind words. :) What really pushed me over the edge
into the abyss of discussion group moderation responsibilities was
returning home after a wonderful Christmas dinner with family and
friends, to find 13 messages from the FBM . . . only one of which had
any (intelligent) relationship to the Bardon work! At that moment, I
realized that my desire for a focused group out-weighed my reluctance to
take on the responsibilities of being a moderator. Within an hour, I
had the BardonPraxis group set up. ;-)

I hope the synchronicity of the BardonPraxis being born on a night in
which much of the world celebrates the emergence of a new and
significant life, will add to its momentum. So far, it seems to be,
seeing as there are *already* over 40 members! Its spirit is strong and
so far I haven't had to reject a single off-topic post. :)

My best to you,
