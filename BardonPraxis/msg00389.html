group mind", as described on "Bardoncompagnon". 
To my surprise, I start having some problems of vizualisation, in 
the sense that a certain type of mind/people (always the same) is 
reluctant to adhere to the tree of life, and manifest a life of 
their own in my own meditation !! I feel/see them as "cavemen" 
(sorry - I know this sounds farfetched), who prefer to stay in some 
black structure of theirs. I took the hand of some of them to lead 
them to the structure, but they were extremely reluctant. As if they 
were not watching the same movie.

different mental state fill the sphere each time, apart those 
bizarre "cavemen" who appear each time when everybody else has 
adhered to the tree of life.

My question is : ...is this something I should correct? 
Should I start again till I am able to get/maintain the script I 
want, or should I just let it happen ?

aspect however. I'm distracted for other reasons :-) although things
