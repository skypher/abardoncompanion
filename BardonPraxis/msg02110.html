Dear Friends,

Many of you are familiar with Yogi Ramacharaka's
Science of Breath. I've seen many recommendations on
this list as well as Rawn Clark's abc.com.

I don't recall ever really seeing anyone connecting
Franz Bardon and Ramacharaka's Yogi Philosophy and
Oriental Occultism. There are so many similarities
that I ask myself if FB wasn't to some extent
influenced by Yogi Ramacharaka's work. The book was
published I believe in 1904.

This book seems to answer many questions about mind,
mental bodies, astral spheres, etc. that IIH may have
been vague about. 

Since I am far from completing the IIH steps, I don't
recognize the subtle differences, if any, in the
teachings, but I'll leave that to the reader. I just
wanted to point people to this resource:

http://www.arfalfa.com/YogiPhilosphy/YogiPhilosophy.htm

If anyone has the "Hatha Yoga" .pdf I would appreciate
a link. The copyright is expired on these books, so
it's all legal. :-)

Sincerely,
Allen Kray
