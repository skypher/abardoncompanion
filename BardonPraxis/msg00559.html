Something else *new*, now.

At a first glance, those exercises in step II *seem* to be a bit 
easier for me than the ones in step I. The reason is probably that I 
am more used to the techniques involved. But something really new 
occured - afterwards.

I had gone through the mental exercise and through the "40-knots-
meditation", and I was on my way to get asleep. 
Half-way between the state of simple "drowsyness" and real sleep, I 
felt as-if the periphery of my entire body (1-2 centimeters outside) 
had been switched on, and had become extremely energetic. It was not 
like a flash, but more like a lamp or a neon tube, although it was 
extremely sudden, almost brutal as a feeling. 
An obvious notion of light. I did not see anything :-) but the whole 
event integrated the notion of light - don't know why.

The area was roughly the same as the one I feel during the 2nd 
exercise of the Archaeous, meaning the physical feedback of the 
astral body, with the difference of a greater intensity and 
suddenness.

Needless to say that it woke me up each time. It happened 3-4 times, 
just last night.

Any meaning to all this ?
