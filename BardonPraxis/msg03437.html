+++ No tip, but a reflection:
As much as there are diffrent kinds of *love*, there must be 
different expressions of *will* as well.
AS far as it concerns my own aspiration, there is absolutely *no* aim 
to "making the ego bow down completely to my will" because this 
*sounds* for me like the ego-mind itself, wanting to control 
everything out of fear and not out of love. 
For myself - when development of will-power goes along with 
spontaneous expression of will (without doubt) as well as with a 
loving attitude, transcending fear more and more, it feels for me as 
the right direction.
