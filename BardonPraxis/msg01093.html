What helped me at first was you imagine this one thing and either make it move 
(your mind will find it difficult to have a static image) so have it get bigger 
smaller, spin, and such. Also you can thin kof stuff that is general as in love 
and think of things that have close connection with love and/or think of love 
around you in you spread to others etc. This has to be narrowed down eventually 
until its just love. This gives your mind a little breathing room and prepares 
it to hold the one thing.
