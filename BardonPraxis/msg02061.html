Dear Dan ~

As Bardon's system is basically solitary, you will have difficulty 
finding such organizations. Bardon's system works on the principle of 
the Self, unlike most ceremonial systems which are based on 
hierarchial initiation.

The first steps of IIH, where the novice might actually need support 
and guidance, are all covered through this group and the group owner 
Rawn Clark's excellent site www.abardoncompanion.com. You will find 
that as you progress through the steps, this sort of guidance will 
become less and less useful or needed, as experiential knowledge 
grows and "fills in the gaps" all on its own.

Well met
Vovin
