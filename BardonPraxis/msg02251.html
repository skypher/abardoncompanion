Hello,
A "cold shower" is not necessary. All that is necessary is a blast of cold water. The directions, in essence, say to bathe or wash in cold water. The footnote mentions that a cold shower can take its place.

When showering all that is necessary is to turn the water to cold for a short while. I usually do about 30 seconds to a minute and then resume the normal temperature. You might try doing this one or two times per shower. This technique is actually used by some therapists to increase circulation.
