Hello all,

My name is Max. I am 33 and live in the Southeastern United 
States. I have been a student of the occult since I was around 15. 
My interests began with traditional witchcraft and quickly moved to 
ceremonial magick based on Crowley and the Golden Dawn. Later on in 
my life I migrated to the 'Dark Side' and embraced Chaos magick. I 
was even a member of the Temple of Set for a short while. I then 
abandoned magick/occult for a while and discovered Bardon. His 
approach was unlike anything I had experienced before. It was 
straightforward and logical. Actually I am starting over and 
beginning with IIH. I hope to find guidance in this group. I would 
welcome any correspondence with others just beginning this path. I 
am a good self started, but often need a kick in the pants to 
continue. Thanks for your time.


Max
