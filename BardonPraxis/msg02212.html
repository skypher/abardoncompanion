Hi David,

> It still confuses me. I can, for instance, generate a certain 
state; 
> say my wish is "openness." I can cause myself to imagine and 
> experience openness. But then I have to imagine that it is outside 
> myself, and then bring it in again. This confuses me. How do I do 
> this without making the assumption of deficiency that I just 
> described, but also without making the breathing-in irrelevant? 
> Someone else described it as like charging a battery, which makes a 
> little more sense, but I would appreciate any clarification.

Perhaps another metaphor may be weight-lifting; you've done a nice 
job of selecting a *quality* as your goal that will make this easier. 
In fact, an individual human seems to be almost a hologram; we have 
in ourselves all possible qualities in vestigial or more or less 
developed states. When we lift weights we always start with *some* 
strength and working with externals we enhance and develop this 
quality towards a personal objective. The interaction of the person 
and the weight creates an emergent effect.

By projecting conscious intent out onto a "tunable" medium that loops 
back in through an assimilation circuit, we can discover a process of 
leverage and feedback, of riding on a natural- magical pattern for 
change, that is difficult (and requires different technique) to 
create in-situ. We don't manufacture more force we attract/tap more 
energy resonant with our intent. At least that's how I think of it. 

One key I've found consistently useful is exactly the approach you 
are taking: finding a metaphor that clicks with your own personal 
history for making magic your own. The flip side is when metaphors 
we've been conditioned with in mundane reality do our thinking for us 
and create obstacles to exploring new ways to interact with 
perception. An eye-opening account of this almost invisible process 
can be found in a book called "Metaphors we Live by." An entertaining 
read.

Another key is the charging seems to work like a generator, once a 
sufficient baseline is established, the state seems to pull energy, 
events and circumstances that maintain and enhance what we've 
started. It seems we begin sending out "vibes" that create new 
resonances in a positive feedback loop.
