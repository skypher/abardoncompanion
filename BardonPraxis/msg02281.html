Dear Friends,

In keeping with a "tradition" I began last year, I will again be taking
the entire month of March off as a holiday from my online duties.
Starting Monday, March 1st, "Vekelim" has kindly agreed to take over the
responsibility of moderating the BardonPraxis and TMO-WG yahoo
discussion groups in my absence. :)

For me, March is a time of added introspection, relaxation, recreation
and reaffirming the inspiration that keeps me doing what I do. I have
plans for a bit of travel, several artistic projects, beginning work in
my vegetable garden, enjoying the emergence of Spring and immersing my
body in the healing mineral waters of my favorite local hotsprings. :)

While I will still be periodically keeping myself appraised of what
transpires online, my responses and involvement will be minimal to
non-existent during the whole of March. That means if you write to me
personally, you can expect a delay in my reply of up to four weeks. I
will reply to truly urgent messages and will fill all orders for CDs and
books as rapidly as humanly possible. The only other commitment I will
be sustaining throughout the month is my ongoing work with the TMO
Working Group.

My best to you all,
:) Rawn Clark
