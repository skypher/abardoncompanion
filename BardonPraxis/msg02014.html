Dear all,

I wanted to share with you an experience .

It dawned on me that Christmas is more than the birth of Christ for those 
christians amongst you. It is the embodiment of light. A little like when 
Neendarthal man disovered the power of fire and then went on to try and master 
it. Christmas, whatever the religion, it so happens that is what it is for the 
christians but I am sure other religions have the equivalent, Hanukah etc..., 
any way it is the Birth of Light in our world, since it always existed 
elsewhere . And it is light that brightens the darkness in our souls.Let there 
be Light ! Remember! It is the light that shows the way and puts us on a higher 
plane. At this time we should feel more atuned with waht Bardon calls Divine 
Providence because Divine Providence is the ultimate embodiment of all things , 
and since so much psychic energy is turned and concentrated on this 
enlightnment we should be in a better position to open ourselves and receive it 
in our deepest like boarding a plane ready to take off.

I know I may sound like a priest or a minister or something, but when one sees 
the happiness in the eyes of the children around you, one realizes that the 
true meaning of this enlightnment is to bring you peace and happiness on your 
path. That spark in their eyes is the spark in the Universe. Look at it, 
penetrate it and let it permeate you.

And all of a sudden it occured to me. More than a Christmas tree, presents and 
eating, and church going for those who do. It became the ultimate physical 
incarnation, Light in our heart! And this is irrespective of any religion which 
all strive towards the enlightnment and have similar manifestations of the 
Birth of Light. 

The Embodiment of Light.

In my opinion, this summarizes the Bardon path.

Jay
