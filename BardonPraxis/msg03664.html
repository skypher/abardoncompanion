The equipoise is between the elements, not between the negative and positive. 
For example in the white soul mirror say you have 20 Fire, 21 Water, 20 Air and 
20 Earth then that would be balanced. While 10 Fire, 21 Water, 7 Air and 17 
Earth would indicate imbalances.

Also, you are not denying yourself, you are transforming qualities that no 
longer serve you, are destructive, etc (hence being negative). In this way you 
are freeing yourself from denial. You may still retain some of the negative 
quality, but it is used appropriately so it becomes something constructive 
(hence positive). As you polarise toward the postive, you climb up the degrees 
of vibration.
