I am trying to come to terms with white vs. black. From what I read 
and observe, different people define things differently. The most 
strict definition I have seen is that anything done for self or 
against others is black, while only positive things done for mankind 
are white. Another version is that something must be intended to harm 
to be bad. If the practitioner believes he is doing good all is well. 
Wut what if bad comes of something intended to be positive?? 
Meanwhile, I believe it is Dion Fortune who wrote that anything 
derieved other from Kether bears karmic consequences. And the Unity 
Church where I go sometimes talks freely about "manifesting" material 
things. To me this seems questionable. I am confused. My instinct is 
toward the more strict definition, but I am really not sure. I cannot 
recall any of Bardon's commentary. Rawn, have you every written 
anything on this topic...or do you have any thoughts? Or anyone?

Blessings to all,
