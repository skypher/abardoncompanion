You have to include all the negative traits since your childhood. Try 
to think back to your first memories, about your negative emotions, 
about things that you did to hurt others or yourself, even if you 
don't do those things anymore. Do that and you'll go 200+. That's the 
easy part. The hard part is to classify them into Fire, Water, Air, 
Earth. That's how you'll learn about your elemental balance. What is 
lacking and what is in excess. It's a pretty damn good exercise if 
you ask me. You can get more help by getting your zodiac done by this 
guy. I forgot his name and website, perhaps someone else remembers 
here. Rawn also recommends getting it done for help in soul mirror 
exercise.
