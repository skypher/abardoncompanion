The three books that Bardon authored are based on the first three 
Tarot cards. INITIATION INTO HERMETICS corresponds to the MAGICIAN, 
PRACTICE OF MAGICAL EVOCATION corresponds to THE HIGH PRIESTESS, and 
THE KEY TO THE TRUE QUABBALA corresponds to THE EMPRESS. Had the 
Communist police not requistioned the tapes of the Fourth Book which 
was to be called THE GOLDEN BOOK OF WISDOM which would have 
corresponded to THE EMPEROR, a fragment of this book can be found at 
the end of FRABATO, THE MAGICIAN. Bardon was on a Divine mission to 
reveal this scared wisdom, but only up to the fifth card. As this is 
explained in the later part of FRABATO, the MAGICIAN. There is no 
other material other than what is presently published.
