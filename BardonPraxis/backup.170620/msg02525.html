Dear Rawn,

Let's assume a person becomes a very proficient step 10 initiate, 
who is also very proficient in mathematics. Would it be possible for 
that adept to design a mathematical model of the universe, just like 
modern physics, but which also includes the planes above the 
physical as well?
Based on such a model, a person who is capable of operating in both 
the physical and the higher planes would be able to build devices 
that have effects ranging beyond the physical. Perhaps doing 
something similar to - for example - loading a talisman, but doing 
so not relying mainly on the crafter's intuition and innate 
understanding of the universal laws, but on precise mathematical 
modeling.
I assume that even if it is possible, it would not be possible to 
model the universe beyond the abyss.

Are there any thoughts you can share about this subject?
