Dear Friends,

Well, the sun has just set on Autumn here and as usual, at Solstice and
Equinox, I bring you all a gift. :) My gift today is the completion of
a major opus, of something very dear to my heart -- the Self-Healing
Archaeous audio series of Lessons.

** New Lesson Nine of the Self-Healing Archaeous **
Yesterday, I recorded the final (yes, it *really* is the final
one this time!) Lesson Nine: The Fine Art of Integration, which
completed the series. This Lesson, which concerns the important magical
Art of integration, ties the whole Archaeous together. Discussion and
practice focus upon three categories of integration: integrating the
fruits of astra-mental wanderings, integrating the lessons of mental
wanderings and integrated Self-expression (or Physical-Astra-Mental
Wandering). As usual, the script of this recording is also available, in
.html, .pdf and .lit formats. All of these can be found by visiting
http://www.ABardonCompanion.com/Archaeous-Links.html

** New Audio-CD = Self-Healing Archaeous: Part Three **
Now that Lesson Nine is finished and the audio series complete,
I've issued a third audio-CD. Part Three, "Elemental Balancing,
Wandering and Integration", contains Lessons Six, Seven, Eight and Nine.
This one, like all of my CD's, can be ordered by visiting my CD's page.
http://www.ABardonCompanion.com/CDs.html

** New DATA-CD **
THE COMPLETE AUDIO RECORDINGS
By Rawn Clark
"The Center of Stillness Meditation"
"Rawn's Self-Healing Archaeous"
"The Magic of IHVH-ADNI"
Several months ago, a visitor asked why I hadn't issued a
data-CD containing all my audio recordings in MP3 format. My excuse at
the time was that I had yet to complete The Magic of IHVH-ADNI and
Self-Healing Archaeous series of Lessons, but I promised that as soon as
those were complete, I'd issue such a compilation. Well, now that both
series are finished, here's the fulfillment of that promise! :)
This CD is ideal for use on a personal computer. It contains
the complete collection of all my audio recordings in .mp3 format, plus
the scripts for each recording in Adobe Acrobat .pdf format. It has a
simple .html interface (Menu.html) which is easily opened by any web
browser, providing quick and logical access to all the audio and text
files.
This CD comes with an 8 page booklet-insert and each copy is
