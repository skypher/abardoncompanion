The following is what I term a "guide dog" for the period between the
summer solstice and the autumn equinox of 2003. It is, essentially, a
very general guide to the astrological energy surrounding and within the
Earth Zone during that period, with specific emphasis upon the work of
initiation for students of magic.
It is not to be completely trusted! A guide dog doesn't understand what
manner of vehicles are rushing past, but it knows when not to cross the
road! Each individual self will experience these effects individually,
and we may also see some of these energies playing out within the global
arena.

This leads me to several important points.

Astrology and Time

Astrology, and the birth chart, is a living, ever changing entity. From
the moment something is born, the horoscope does not rest, it vibrates,
and begins ticking away like a clock, each of the points or planets like
a hand keeping a different time; for instance, the Sun takes 1 year to
travel around the whole zodiac wheel, and therefore acts as an annual
marker, whilst some others take much longer to complete a circle, and
some much less, the moon being the shortest (28 days, or one month, the
root of the word "moon" being connected to the word "month"), and Pluto
the longest (taking about 270 years to make circuit). As these planets
wander 'around' the horoscope, they come across the places other planets
inhabited in the birth chart, ('points' within the field of the Earth
Zone which we are highly sensitised to) and their energies trigger them
off, like the way in which the tumblers in a safe click into place when
the combination is entered, revealing the contents. Such an event is
called a transit.

It is through studying when the transits occur that astrologers make
their predictions. It is an extremely precise method of so called
'prediction', but it requires the application of intuitive vision on the
part of the astrologer to get right, since the planets are rich in
symbolic content, and express themselves in many different, yet
synchronistically connected, ways, as you would expect in a world where
everything is unique.

In drawing up this guide, I have no individual horoscope to work from,
and have instead taken the actual zodiacal positions of the planets and
related them to the other actual zodiacal positions. In other words, I
am looking into the space-time relationships that the planets in the
heavens are making to each other throughout this period. The planets I
have used for this work are Jupiter, Chiron, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune,
Pluto and the Lunar Nodes, the head and tail of the cosmic Dragon. These
planets, known as the outer planets, are describing the greater cosmic
forces exerting themselves over all life on Earth. I have also included
some solar aspects, particularly around the solstice and equinox, to
illustrate how these work to influence each day (it is from the
transiting aspects the Sun makes to other planets each day that most of
those daily horoscope columns are made from.)

Time itself is not linear, but a continuum within a single point, like
light; birth is not the beginning, and death not the end, it is perhaps
more accurate to say that birth and death are the beginning and end of
time itself. When we are born into the physical world, we 'enter' the
space - time continuum, and when we die, we 'leave' it. The horoscope
you are born with, or which something shares its moment with, describes
this journey. The concept of non-linear time may seem odd at first, with
the emphasis we place on the stages of maturity and growth through life,
and our perception of a beginning, a middle, and an end; but the truth
is, life is the process of becoming that which we already are. Just as a
straight line is an optical illusion and becomes a segment or arc of a
circle when extended indefinitely, just as matter when closely observed
resolves into orbiting electrical charges, and the galaxy rotates in a
stately waltz about the universe which, no doubt, also itself spirals
upon an axis as it expands, so too the life of one human being and
social principles follow circular-spiralic paths. The trick is, knowing
and being where you are on the spiral, in relation to the whole.

My Guide Dog report is a pathfinder. It pinpoints exactly where we all
are, collectively (and therefore only generally) and projects that path
into the future, or even the past, illuminating the entire journey from
our present now perspective.

Astrologers (and magicians) are often asked about the conflict between
free will and fate. Do we have free will to determine our destiny, or is
this a clockwork universe where everything is preordained, and
inescapable? This is a vital question that demands an answer. Every
astrologer will give you a different answer (as they themselves reflect
their charts viewpoint on life), but it is my feeling that we are given
the freedom to do what we will with our life as co-creators. The only
thing we have no power to change is our essence, our spiritual or divine
nature. Certain things are part of our life path, however, and we cannot
avert them, because it is our spiritual will to face them; furthermore,
because of the cyclic/spiralic nature of creation, all charts contain
(sometimes titanic) struggles and challenges which recur repeatedly
through time and space. However, and this is key, we are always free to
react in any way we wish. Through our behaviour, we can actually change
our manifest destiny, for as we learn to perceive our world and our life
as but reflections of one another, just as the planets and other realms
mirror events on Earth, we can through soul alchemy release the chains
which bind us to the nonexistent past and future, and live for the Now,
where we can proceed along the path of enlightened understanding,
embrace pain as the door to healing and wholeness, and join our song
with the eternal dance and symphony of the stars.

When the wolf howls a cry of life at the Moon, She calls out to all the
pack a song of the Oneness of all, that stirs the soul, and brings forth
the memory that 'character is fate', all of Nature is whole and
interconnected, that the path and the traveller are the same as the end
destination, that it is our ultimate destiny to fully exercise our free
will, and discover who we really are.

First some general pointers about the above:

· Note how Jupiter travels quickly through the latter
half of the Fixed Fire sign, Leo ('the span of interpretation').This is
excellent for expansive working on all Fire related issues and magical
workings, particularly those which relate to self confidence, fame,
recognition, honesty, generosity, creative energy and the libido or
pro-creative energy. Any over-abundance or dearth of these qualities
will make itself known. Jupiter enters Virgo on Aug 27th, cooling things
down considerably after that point, and initiating a phase of growth
through detached analysis and discriminatory intelligence (lasting to
Oct 2004). Thus there is a shift from Fire to Earth.

· Saturn is our cosmic timepiece. For this reason its
aspects at the beginning of the season contain the magical essence of
that time. Saturn's presence in Cancer emphasises our ties and
responsibilities to family, the watery (family, nurturing) environment,
and the deep astral past. Saturn has an important and esoteric role to
play in this time span that most will not detect, so I will spell it out
here - it begins the season at the solstice with a trine to Uranus, and
ends the season at the equinox with an opposition to Chiron; thus, in
the beginning there is great opportunity to build a new (group)
mentation, but by September (the 26th, just after the equinox), all our
woundedess and weaknesses are about us, pressing in.

· Chiron's retro position in mid Capricorn forces the
healing of broken rules, regulations and strictures, our outdated modes
of government and issues of social status worldwide. It reaches a zenith
of power when it opposes Saturn (Sept 26th) and we will feel this coming
when it turns direct on Sept 19th.

· Note also Uranus in Pisces, a relatively new
vibration. This awakens the collective unconscious, putting us in touch
with the ocean of emotion. Previously, we have all been operating on a
heightened mental level of awakening. This is now shifting to an astral
dimension of awakening, but Uranus is actually bridging the 2
(astra-mental) when it retrogrades back to Aquarius.

· Finally, individuals born in mid Sagittarius of any
year (a few days either side of and around the 7th of December; hello
Tina!) pay close attention to Pluto, a planet which has been behind
literally world altering events in your life and is gearing up for a big
transformation...many of you born around this time will be nodding your
heads right now, thinking back over the last year or so, and those of
you born in Sagittarius from Dec 7th- 22nd or so, look up! Pluto is
coming for you! Life is about to never be the same again.
