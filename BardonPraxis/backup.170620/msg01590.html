Dear Friends,

Having received 500 responses after 14 months, the Bardon Questionnaire
is now closed. I wish to thank all those who helped make the
Questionnaire possible and to all who took the time to respond! :)

The online "community" of Bardonists now have a valuable statistical
resource! If you're wondering how long it took folks to complete a
specific Step, you now have a place to look for that information. If
you're wondering how many folks have read which books or have which
interests, you now have a place to research such information. If you
want to know the age ranges, genders or countries of online Bardonists,
then here's the place to look. :)

If you want a quick and easy view of the 500 responses, broken down by
category and number of responses per category (this is the simplest way
to view the results), then visit the "Report" page.

If you wish for a detailed view which displays each individual response
and allows for true research, then you will need to visit five separate
pages, each containing 100 results.

Or, you can download all these pages at once in a .ZIP file.

Each of these options is available by visiting
http://www.ABardonCompanion.com/Question/Links.html

My best to you,
:) Rawn Clark
