Dear BardonPraxis members,

After ten years of involvement with online discussion groups answering
questions and commenting about the practice of Bardon's system of
initiation, it is time for me to retire from that aspect of my work. :)

Over the past year, I have noted that the vast majority of practical
questions asked and replies given have already been addressed on my
website and, in essence, I have been merely repeating things I've stated
in the past. So it is time for me to devote my time and energies to
more productive pursuits such as writing my next book and composing
other new works that have been repeatedly put off due to lack of time.

So, as of May 1st, I will no longer be involved with any online
discussion groups except those pertaining to The Magic of IHVH-ADNI.
Furthermore, I will be limiting the amount of private Bardon-related
correspondence that I engage in by removing my email address from my
website and substituting a web-based form.

As a consequence, the BardonPraxis will be closing at the end of April.
This will give those who wish, time to download the BardonPraxis
archives and time for others to do a final search of the archives. And
as of April 15th I will disallow any new members from joining the
BardonPraxis.

My best to you all,
:) Rawn Clark
