Dear Friends,

I'm happy to introduce http://www.tmo-wg.net !!! "The Magic of
IHVH-ADNI" (a.k.a., TMO), now has its very own Internet home, dedicated
solely to TMO!!! This website is presented in English and French.

Since I introduced the audio series of TMO Lessons last year, I have
come to realize just how separate TMO is from the Bardon-oriented work
on ABardonCompanion.com. And with the recent advent of the TMO Working
Group, I finally decided it was time to create a new website, just for
TMO.

Not only does http://www.tmo-wg.net serve as home for TMO, it is also
the Internet Portal for the TMO Working Group. Here one can learn about
TMO, download the audio Lessons or the textual scripts, order the CD's,
learn about the TMO Working Group and even apply to join the Working
Group.

Furthermore, I have created a new Yahoogroup for discussing all aspects
of "The Magic of IHVH-ADNI". For some time now, it has concerned me
that the BardonPraxis and the FranzBardonMagi groups are where the
majority of discussion about TMO occurs, and since TMO is not, strictly
speaking, a Bardon-related topic, I felt it was time to open a new
discussion group dedicated just to TMO. You can join by visiting
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/TMO-WG or by sending an email to
TMO-WG-Subscribe@yahoogroups.com


My best to you,
:) Rawn Clark
