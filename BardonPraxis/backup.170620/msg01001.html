Dear Rawn,

This morning I sorta came to a little bit of a revelation with 
regards to the third tarot key. My mother is starting to work at 
cultivate and hybridize daylillies and was looking at the colours of 
the colour wheel to look at how the colours combine, mix and so 
forth. Being a painter, and me having half an ear open, she gave me a 
little bit of a lecture on the three primary colours, and the three 
secondary colours, and then how colours on the opposite side of the 
colour wheel are complimentary to each other.

A couple minutes after going through this with my mother, I turned to 
the tarot key...and sure enough: it's just a large colour wheel! All 
this time I've been searching for some complicated connection between 
the symbols and what not all and the answer was right there in front 
of me where Bardon said to meditate upon the colour symbolism. So 
simple and yet so complex...

Love and Live well,
Peter Reist
