Dear Alexandra,

>> When working with water to draw away weaknesses and filth from body
and soul,
Bardon makes it very clear, to immediately dispose off the water, or use
running water. However, when it comes to the magnetic eye bath, he does
not say to pour the water down the drain or elsewhere. <<

Because with the magnetic eye bath, he is not suggesting that you do
this to rid yourself of anything. Instead he suggests this only as a
means of gaining something from the bath -- i.e., the wish you've
impregnated the water with. So, you are not excreting anything negative
into the water.

>> Also it is not clear to me, whether I should do the eye rolling
exactly 7 times, or is *7* the minimum number? <<

Doesn't matter.

productive? <<

questionable.
