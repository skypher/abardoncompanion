Dear Barry,

>> My question relates to Astral Ether as it is mentioned in Step VI.
Just what is Astral Ether? On the one hand, Bardon seems to relate it to
vital energy in that it can be accumulated in astral and physical
bodies, but on the other hand he relates it to Akasha, which cannot be
accumulated. Is Astral Ether quadripolar like vital energy, or is it
the manifestation of the causal element in the astral plane? <<

The "astral ether" is Bardon's term here for the Akasha at an astral
density or level ('akasha' and 'ether' being, at times, interchangeable
terms). Here, the Akasha manifests with astral features such as color
and sensation (perceived with the *astral* senses), but it is still
Akasha. Therefore, the 'astral ether' *cannot* be accumulated (the
exercises involve breathing it in and out but not accumulation).
