Bardon, in his third book, says that Divine Providence has only 
allowed him to reveal three of the Tarot cards Arcanum. but he 
wishes that he could at least do two more. Since Bardon is gone, 
maybe his students know what he was refering to, but I haven't read 
anything to that exact effect. The question is, at that level 
(after mastering the thrid Tarot secrets) does one no longer need 
any instruction and instead receives instruction from the Guardian 
Genius? or from other Beings? or from Divine Providence?
And if that's the case, then someone should at least know what the 
topic of those Tarot cards are. So why hasn't someone continued 
Bardon's work, presuming that Divine Providence allowed it? For 
Bardon saw it as being beneficial to higher adepts that had 
progressed that far, so it must have been worth the effort to do so.
