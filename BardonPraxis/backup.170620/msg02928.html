Hello all,

I would like to invite all serious students of Hermetics to join the
recently opened HermeticResearch.Org portal, and participate in bringing
under one roof the best material on the subject and discuss both the
theoretical and practical sides on the forum.

About Hermetic Research:
Hermetic Research is not an initiatic school. It's not a religion. It's not
a sect. It's not a secret society. It's not a conspiracy group, nor does it
seek to diffuse conspiracy theories. It's not a place where there are
Masters and followers. It's not a place to establish any kind of hierarchy.
Nor is it a place to gain power or status. The goals of Hermetic Research
are not to start a revolution of any kind against any thing; nor to
establish any kind of dogma.

Hermetic Research is about Freedom and Truth; it's about having the freedom
of mind for everyone in order to seek their own Truth. It's goals are not to
spread "A" light, but to awake the light that lies inside each of us. It is
not meant to offer certainties but to provide inspiration for those who want
to pursue them.

Thank you very much for your time.

In Light and Truth,

Hermetic Research Team
