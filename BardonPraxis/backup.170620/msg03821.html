+++ I've learnt a certain beginners way of contacting PME Spirits by 
German bardonists magicians (smiliar as Mistele says, everyone "a 
little bit" psychic may contact them) or the way of Haziel, having 
practised this for some time. (I know that - although all those 
teachers having all worked with IIH, PME and Kabbalah of Bardon - 
they still have *different* approaches.) 
The reason for not focusing on this practise now is less because I 
would think it does not work at all neither being totally wrong (and 
whether it is an evocational practise or not, I cannot say yet - 
neither knowing about the Abramelin and PME enough yet), but rather 
the effect seemed not to be solid, not to be grounded enough for me.

>From your Bardon approach, I've learnt the value of the very basic 
exercises, not enough being aware of this before .

They are now even fascinating and it is a joy intensifying them.
I do not feel a need for practising external "candle" focusing 
anymore, instead just the first exercise "thought-observing" is very 
rich concerning mental discipline.

It seems, doing this way, once learnt a faculty - smiliar as riding 
a bycicle - you will keep this quite naturally when going on, 
whereas a lot of modern spiritual paths or even kundalini kind of 
meditations have a quick bliss-effect but are not solid or do not 
always work as real transformation, having tendency fading away 
somehow.

The most important thing at all - for myself - concerning this way, 
is that it does not boost a spiritual super Ego, but 
you are even better related, grounded and cordial connected to daily 
life and people. And this is absolut extraordinary, I've never found 
this before in such a way - and therefore, not raising kundalini or 
such things are "special" first of all, but personally, living a 
more balanced life is the *real wonder* striving for.

Thanks for all your hints and inspirations
Patrik
