Dear Olivier

Welcome to Bardon Praxis from me, a very new newbie, but quite an old 
man! I am English and too, stumbled across the work of Bardon, which 
I find absolutely fascinating and very, very rewarding. I am finding 
it hard to practice, like you, as I have a very busy life, and quite 
young children (I have a much younger wife), but I cant give it up. I 
found Bardon because, as a meditator and Buddhist I started to do 
work on chakras and came across the western idea of elements, got 
into Bardon and was hooked.

Also like you, my partner isn't interested in Bardon, and is a little 
wary. She thinks I'm weird! But she comes from South Africa and has 
seen people enslaved by their fear of muti, the magical 
superstitions, and power of the witchdoctor. So, I tell her I am 
still, like her, a good buddhist, but am exploring, and she sees 
that I haven't changed, am not whacking black goats heads off and 
offering up the entrails to Beelzebub, and for the rest, well, Bardon 
enjoins silence and secrecy as a main practice in IIH, and I find it 
empowering not to discuss it with anyone else. It weakens one's 
practice, I feel.

Good luck with IIH. I hope it is your path.


Regards, Uthlagus.
