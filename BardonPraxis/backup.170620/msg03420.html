Maybe the answer lies in your very own words, "bit by bit"?

Value what is small-scale, and before going for "longer term fulfilment",
try short term. 

Change small things, do one detail differently. Use the left hand instead
of the right, use a different word, do things in a different order. First
when it´s easy to do either, in emotionally neutral situations. That might
give you edge and strength when the going gets tough.

I should do this myself more. There are hundreds of occasions every day.
