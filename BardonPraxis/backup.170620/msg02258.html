Dear Allen,

The feeling of "increasing aggressiveness" of the outer world could be due to 
your senses becoming more subtle. Is it, on the other hand, balanced by a 
stronger perception of the "positive" things? 

<<more negative circumstances are being generated>> Do you think the "negative 
circumstances" being generated are purely negative? They might serve to bend 
your mind to certain development.

<<forces are trying their best to get me to quit or break my focus>> 
This problem can be eliminated if:
- There is no worry that something will make you quit or loose the focus, 
otherwise your worry will attract disruptions of all kinds; 
- Exercises are extensively incorporated into your mundane life. There are many 
exercises which do not require special place, time and arrangement. Among them 
all thought control exercises, soul mirror, mental magical speech, etc. Such 
exercises can be done during your busy day.

All best wishes,
L.A.
