Hello, my name is Kiyan, and I have recently developed
a serious interest in the book "Initiation into
Hermetics."

When I was very young, I had an interest in
witchcraft. My mother introduced me to many
psychological methods based on est and NLP. These
were helpful tools, but, of course, lacked a spiritual
foundation. Like many occultists, I came across books
by chance, almost as if they "fell into my lap."

>From very basic witchcraft I shifted to an intense
focus on Crowley. I was fascinated by his intellect
and personality. I've read his autohagiography twice,
and own many of his cornerstone works. Still, his
system is very confusing, and while I use much of his
writing (for instance the Book of Thoth for
interpreting the paths) I find most of his writing
requires too much cross-referencing. Also, I think
his ego gets in the way too often. I still value his
works, however.

Recently I have been studying the Qabalah and working
upon the tree. I plan to continue my quabalistic
studies while I work upon Bardon's initiation
methodology.

I suppose I should mention that I sustain a personal
magic system that is entirely unique, retained since
early childhood. In fact it so unique that there is
no purpose in saying anything else about it. I guess
I want people out there to know it's ok to have their
own magical creations.

I am drawn to Bardon mostly because of a sense of his
earnestness and purity of heart. I hope to fulfill
initiation so I may "love under will" to the utmost of
my power.

Over the next few days I will have many questions and
I hope to use this group to sort through these. If
some may find these questions naive, then so be it. I
still intend to ask these questions. I have searched
the message archives but not found the answers. I
know someone out there can help me.

Well, I have said enough. Fist of Mars is my moniker
because recently I have felt a need to draw upon the
force of Geburah.

Talk to you soon,
