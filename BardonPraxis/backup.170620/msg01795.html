Both ways of pronouncing it are proper or correct since it can be
pronounced either way. :) The 'V' can have several different
pronunciations depending upon it's use and upon the vowel point
associated with it. It also depends upon whether one is speaking
Sephardic or Ashkenazic Hebrew. At any rate, I "grew up" speaking it as
"Vav", but "Vau" is just as proper.
