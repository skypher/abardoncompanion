know), and I've come to realise that there are literally 1000s of 
spirits hanging around the earth and individuals (particularly those 
involved with magic) wanting to attune and aid people to help 
themselves and the earth. Its there job and they want to do it! 
Bardon had a lot of these guys on team, and now we all have an 
oppurtunity to create this kind of relationship with the spirit 
world. It wants to happen. Many of us are being called to wield the 
kind of power that Bardon possesed. Dont ever lie to yourself about 
who you are. Please reply to this message with relevant experiences,
