Dear Friends,

It's with great satisfaction and pleasure that I announce the release of
a new Lesson in "The Magic of IHVH-ADNI" audio series! :)

LESSON SEVEN: Setting the Tone of the Temporal Moment

This is an advanced usage of The Magic of IHVH-ADNI in which the
practitioner creates conditions within the material realm that are in
harmony with the realization of a specific desire. The technique
presented here, serves as the foundation for the next two Lessons which
will be released soon.

Unlike the previous audio Lesson in this series, I've split Lesson Seven
into two parts: an 'Introduction' and a 'Practice'. With the
'Introduction' (run time of 19:21 and a file size of 11 MB), I explain
the technique and provide a detailed description of one example from my
own personal work of "setting the tone". And in the 'Practice' section
(run time of 23:17 and a file size of 13.3 MB), I guide the listener
through the process, step by step.

As usual, I've posted a .ZIP file containing both .MP3 files (file size
of 23.3 MB) and have also posted the script of this audio recording in
.LIT (Microsoft Reader eBook) and .PDF (Adobe Reader) formats for your
convenience.

All of these files can be downloaded from my "Updates" page by visiting
http://www.ABardonCompanion.com/Updates.html
