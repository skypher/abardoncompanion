> As a step in true initiation, Evocation is a practice which expands 
the
> *magician's* awareness. As the magician evokes an entity they are
> simultaneously expanding their own self-awareness to the point at 
which
> it then encompasses the aspect of the planetary zone that the entity
> personifies. When this *truly* occurs, all of the attributes 
(powers,
> abilities, etc.) that the entity personifies become aspects of the
> magician's own self and the magician is then able to wield those 
powers,
> abilities, etc. for themselves. At first, this will be perceived 
as an
> Ankur (i.e., as the entity immediately passing their attributes to 
the
> magician) but in truth, this is not an Ankur but rather, is due to 
the
> magician's own expansion of self. And with accumulated experience, 
the
> magician comes to distinguish between the true Ankur and the
> self-directed expansion of their own awareness.
> 
> Some entities possess the special ability to pass a true Ankur to a
> magician who is not yet capable of expanding their own self 
awareness
> (i.e., a magician who has not yet reached the level of maturity
> required). In effect, this forces an expansion of the magician's
> awareness and can, in some cases, be detrimental to magician.
> 
> Most entities however, will *reveal* information to an immature 
magician
> instead of forcing them to expand with an Ankur. In this case, the
> magician must apply what is learned in order to gradually expand 
their
> awareness sufficiently to wield the abilities the entity has exposed
> them to (i.e, an IIH-like process).
