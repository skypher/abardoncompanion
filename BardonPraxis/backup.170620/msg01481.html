I'm very happy to announce the following Updates:

1) "Permutations de l'Arbre: LIVRE 231"
French Translation of "Permutations of the Tree: BOOK 231"
Traduction © 2003 by Tzidaos

A few days ago I received a wonderful surprise in my email - a French
translation of my magnum opus, BOOK 231!! I immediately set to work
converting the text in the existing pages of BOOK 231 to this new French
version and have now posted it on ABC.com! Both the original English and

will likely produce a French version of the BOOK 231 CD as a mate to the
English version CD.

Thank you Tzidaos! :)
