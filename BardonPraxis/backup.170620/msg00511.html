> So it's too late to "out-empath" him/her. And how do
> you out-empath flu btw :-) ?

Ouch! Tough question! My recommendation is this: Treat your empathy 
as you would an allergy. What I mean is, expect unforseen and sudden 
reactions. While adopting this attitude, also work on avoiding acting 
on or submitting to impulse. In the case of sniffles coming on, just 
react with indifference, or rather "ahh, it's just a temporary 
reaction". Don't initially resist, but don't submit either, if you 
get my drift.

I know, easier said than done! ;) Anyhow, give it a try.
