Dear Friends,

Today is the first birthday of http://www.tmo-wg.net ! :) Since
opening its e-door last year as the internet portal for The Magic of
IHVH (TMO) Working Group, www.tmo-wg.net has seen amazing growth, as has
TMO in general and the TMO-Working Group in particular. Almost
immediately after it went online in English, much of the website was
made available in French and, just recently, it has expanded to Polish
(including Polish versions of the .mp3 audio Lessons!).

The TMO Working Group (TMO-WG) has likewise seen great growth. During
the year that has passed, the TMO-WG has successfully established itself
as a vital group, working with effective techniques. In June of this
year, the TMO-WG introduced the "TMO Working Group Academy" which is
proving itself to be very efficacious. It has been so successful that
earlier this month, the Academy had its first graduate and a new TMO
Working Group (the "TMO-WG2") has been formed! :)

The past year has also seen great progress in the evolution of TMO
itself with the publication of several new articles (such as "The Final
Form", "Path of Brilliance", "Theory and Practice" and "TMO and the
Archaeous") and ongoing discussion at
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/TMO-WG .

All in all, it has been a banner year for TMO! :)

So, in celebration of the birthday of www.tmo-wg.net, I'd like to offer
the following website updates:

1) Earlier this month, as a birthday gift to the TMO Working Group,
Aleksander Dumala (webmaster of http://www.franzbardon.pl) released
Polish recordings of the nine TMO audio Lessons! This is the first time
that the original English audio Lessons have been translated and
recorded and I am very thankful to Alek for this wonderful gift!!! :)
The .mp3 files may be downloaded at http://www.tmo-wg.net/MP3s-pl.html
Even if you don't speak Polish, it's worth listening to Alek's great
recording voice! ;-)

2) Four new articles:
** "TMO and the Archaeous" http://www.tmo-wg.net/Rawn-03.html
This is an article from May of this year, only now finding its way to
tmo-wg.net. It concerns the integration of the Self-Healing Archaeous
techniques and TMO practice.

** "Path of Brilliance" http://www.tmo-wg.net/Rawn-04.html
In this recent article I discuss a new TMO form called the "Brilliant
Form" which integrates the Kethric Brilliance, the TMO Final Form and
the Self-Healing Archaeous.

** "TMO: Theory and Practice" http://www.tmo-wg.net/Peter-01.html
Peter Reist continues his thoughts with a new segment titled,
"Complimentary Practices".

** "Meditation of an Open Heart"
http://www.tmo-wg.net/Alek-01.html
Aleksander Dumala offers us a wonderful meditation upon beauty and an
open heart, which involves TMO.

3) Several new "Question & Answer" installments in the 'Miscellaneous'
and 'Lessons 1, 3, & 6' sections. http://www.tmo-wg.net/Q-A.html

Happy 1st Birthday www.tmo-wg.net ! ! ! :)

My best to you all,
:) Rawn Clark
