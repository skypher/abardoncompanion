Dear BardonPraxis members,<BR>
<BR>
After ten years of involvement with online discussion groups answering<BR>
questions and commenting about the practice of Bardon's system of<BR>
initiation, it is time for me to retire from that aspect of my work.  :)<BR>
<BR>
Over the past year, I have noted that the vast majority of practical<BR>
questions asked and replies given have already been addressed on my<BR>
website and, in essence, I have been merely repeating things I've stated<BR>
in the past.  So it is time for me to devote my time and energies to<BR>
more productive pursuits such as writing my next book and composing<BR>
other new works that have been repeatedly put off due to lack of time.<BR>
<BR>
So, as of May 1st, I will no longer be involved with any online<BR>
discussion groups except those pertaining to The Magic of IHVH-ADNI.<BR>
Furthermore, I will be limiting the amount of private Bardon-related<BR>
correspondence that I engage in by removing my email address from my<BR>
website and substituting a web-based form.<BR>
<BR>
As a consequence, the BardonPraxis will be closing at the end of April.<BR>
This will give those who wish, time to download the BardonPraxis<BR>
archives and time for others to do a final search of the archives.  And<BR>
as of April 15th I will disallow any new members from joining the<BR>
BardonPraxis.<BR>
<BR>
My best to you all,<BR>
