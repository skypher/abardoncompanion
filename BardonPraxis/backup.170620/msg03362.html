Dear Dustin,

I have a caffeine addiction, mainly because I'm a computer science 
major and spend late nights programming. But the main point is that 
my addiction is caused by something else, procrastination. If I 
didn't procrastinate, I wouldn't have to stay up late.

You already know why you go for food, but the solution won't lie in 
the food. You'll have to fix those other problems that cause you to 
go to food, and then, when the need for food is at a low, you can 
tackle the food problem.

--Alan
