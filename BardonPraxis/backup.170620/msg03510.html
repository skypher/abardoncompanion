>Your mind allowed a useless thought form to gain control of

I really valued, and learned something from, this response of yours. 

>By training selectiveness in this manner, future
>trivial thought forms are prevented from enterring your mind via the
>overall enhanced vibrational rate, and any that can sneak through can
>be instantly recognised and tossed away - keeping valuable mental
>energy for practical uses.

This *selectiveness* seems to be an important part of magic.  

A somewhat banale example from my everyday life: My two cats are, from time
to time, fighting. But before a confrontation actually takes place there
is *hissssssing*. When I hear this sound, a whole scenario unwinds:
thoughts about a catfight, emotions of disharmony, uneasiness, etc.  

But I´ve turned this around. Whenever I hear this sound now, I hold the
idea of Harmony and Love. Also I see in my mind the cats separating from
each other, avoiding a fight.
