Hi, 

I believe the moderator has answered the question with
"..LBRP gives the illusion of sanity by creating a
temporary, artificial equilibrium. It doesn't help
with the task of elemental equipose, but instead
prepares the practitioner with an encounter with the
shadow self." He said that it could be used for a
short while. In my experience it seems like it
strengthens the will, but it actually deepens the
pre-existing denial system by creating sort of a super
ego. There was an underlying emotional deficit
underlying the false confidence. Leaves me feeling
wired, disconnected, socially atrophied. It didn't
happen for the first few years. . . has anyone else
noticed that it drys you out more as time goes on?
