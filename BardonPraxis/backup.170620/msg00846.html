Dear Newbie,

>From my limited knowledge of PME, Bardon seems to use the term 
evokation for two things: (1) any contact with an external spirit and 
(2) proper, formalized evokation. While PME concerns itself primarily 
with the latter form of evokation, there seem to be several points 
within IIH where one is performing the former form of evokation. The 
step five work to get in contact with the greater guardian genious 
would seem to be a prime example of a methodology that could be used 
to contact other spirits as well. Though, in terms of PME, it is just 
preliminary work to a full blown more formal evokation with circle, 
triangle, magical mirror and what not all.

As far as one pointedness and VOM exercises go, they have the 
potential to get you in contact with essential meaning. Which in turn 
gives you a straight conduit for information to pass. Though, 
ultimatly, I would have to say that it is quite a safe practice.
