IOX137,

When I want to relax one of the first things I do is
tighten the muscles I want to relax, because we in
the modern world aren't always aware of our muscles
and bodies "doing" something. So I exagerate the
doing to then let go and surrender into relaxation,
and non doing. I view the esoteric path whether it be
Bardon or Tibetan tantra or whatever, to be
analogous. Visualization, vital energy exercises etc
by doing them consciously bring up into awareness what
we are unconsciously doing and conversely allows us to
consciouly practice non doing. Otherwise when I might
think I am doing nothing, really all of the
subconscious doings go on and on. 
If as the heart sutra suggests form is emptiness and
emptiness is form, all phenomena including psychic
phenomena reveal this reality, but how do "I" respond
to it?
Enough theory, for me in this post this the praxis
list
