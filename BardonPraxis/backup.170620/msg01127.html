Dear Chuck,

>> C, C#, C##, Dbb, Db, D, ... etc.
So, question; would this factor into the discussion? <<

No. Bardon used the western scale. Additionally, the notes he gave
were only meant to aim the initiate in the right tonal direction and
were not meant as rigid guides.

>> Whereas though, I would "presume" by the initiates' internalizing all
of this that within oneself there would respond a natural resonance of
the correct and true note one is working in the realms of. <<

Yes, that is exactly the case. :) The initiate directly perceives the
proper tone as an aspect of the essential meaning of the Letter.
Without this ability to directly perceive the essential meaning of the
Letter and then harmonize your tonal imagination and vocalization with
it, Bardon's list is useless.
