psychiatric patients where a person is in a psychiatric facility, and 
they are having schizophrenic tendencies. This person is given the 
medication and the counseling but is also introduced to a new form of 
learning -philosophically maybe-, which is, on a whole, much more 
holistic.

In short, How do we go about in trying to help repair a violet cord 
that is severely damaged for someone that has their own psychiatric 
dillema that is an inherent trait or brought on by severe trauma or 
drugs?

things that I do to try to help seem to be specific for each person.
