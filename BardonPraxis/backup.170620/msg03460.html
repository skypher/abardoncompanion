will. I will know when lighting the charcoal that the moment the

selfishness. I can attach the act of inhaling the tabacco to

from previously realizing my desire. Perhaps my desire is to do 
better financially at my job. Then i can inhale the idea of being a 
better worker, perhaps finding some flaw in my work ethics, and 
exhale its opposite.

cigarettes. You still have Tabacco-Earth, Fire-Fire, Filter-Water, 
Breath-Air. Far simpler still you could just impregnate the cigarette 
with your desire, and as it burns know its being realized. But the 
Akashic principle can impregnate anything at all so im sure theres
