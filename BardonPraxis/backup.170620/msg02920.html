Dear Kurt,

Using food, water and air, you basically apply to the Elements of Earth, Water 
and Air.
Therefore, you may not achieve similar successes impregnating the same idea via 
the three media. Your intuition will tell you which meduim to use for the 
particular idea.

In practice, whatever meduim you use, it seems to work well if you accompany 
the impregnation with a four-part formula:
- a sentence that states the desirable effect on the physical level;
- then on the astral level (something to deal with emotions);
- then on the mental level (a related mental concept);
- finally, a sentence that states the idea in the present tense. For example, 
*I have good health*.

The rationale behind such an approach is that any idea permeates all the three 
bodies. 

As for the effectiveness of EM on the physical body, it is more likely to 
happen in an indirect way. For example, if one wishes to get rid of a decease, 
they will not get healthy immediately, but will find an excellent medicine 
soon. 

Wishing you every success,

Lidia
