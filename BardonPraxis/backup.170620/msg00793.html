Dear Peter,

>From an Hermetic perspective, the twin-hemisphere idea only goes so far.
It pertains only to those portions of the *brain* that process certain
functions of the Mind. The brain is not the Mind.

It is the Mind which decides which array of functions it will employ and
the brain merely serves as the receptacle of that will. In other words,
the brain doesn't decide to use one hemisphere or another -- the Mind
decides to function in a certain way and this activity causes
corresponding parts of the brain to function in specific ways.

So when science examines the brain and decides that function 'a' belongs
to one hemisphere, and function 'b' to the other, it's really just
tracking the effects of Mind-acting-through-brain.

What's of paramount importance to the magician is the Mind's function,
not the brain's.
