I think, but it's only my opinion, that you should reformulate your 
suggestion.
I think saying "i am XXX" when you know XXX is not true cause a 
conflict.
To avoid the conflict, you may say "every day, i'm more and more XXX".
Then, there is no conflict, cause what you say is in contradiction 
with nothing.

Let's take an example :
someone might say "i am very rich, i am very rich"... but the fact is 
that they are not, and they know it. When they use autosuggestion, 
those suggestions are a reference to the fact they are not rich, and 
then, it creates a conflict. It might be much more efficient to 
say "abondance" in loop, to create conscience of abondance within 
your subconscious mind, and so, in your life. 

This is just a suggestion.

Also, i would like to add something : you create continuously on the 
astral plane. During autosuggestion, you create something on the 
astral plane. But your thoughts and your feelings should not destroy 
what you already created. For example, if you create succes in your 
life with autosuggestion, and all day long you say "i will never do 
anything good", "i will never succeed"... then you simply destroy 
what you construct with autosuggestion.

And finaly, i would advise you to "let go". It's kinda hard to 
explain, but work hard, then, "forget" what you've done, do something 
else... let the universe do it's job. You know, it's kinda the same 
as with elemental working. You ask him to do something, then, you 
stop think of it, you let him do his job. It's the same here.
For example, you can use autosuggestion morning and evening, but 
during the day, avoid thoughts like "will it work ? will it work ? 
will my autosuggestion work ?". Instead think "i'll do my best and 
the results will be the results, that's all".
