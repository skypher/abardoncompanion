Dear Louis,

>> There were some posts a while back that dealt with what happens
hermetically when one uses different kinds of drugs. I was wondering if
using LSD would cause as much damage to the violet cord if one were to
have the mental tenacity to not be filled with delusion while being
intoxicated. <<

While one would still experience the same physical effects, the mental
effect, and therefore any damage to the violet cord which integrates the
solitary mental body with the astral body, could be negated through a
high degree of mental awareness and discipline. However, such a degree
of mental discipline presupposes the ability to create these mental
states (actually, states superior to drug induced states) for oneself
without the ingestion of LSD. It's this ability and the fact that one
would be well aware of the significance of these states and familiar
with them in advance, that subverts any damage to the violet cord. In
other words, damage to the violet cord specifically, arises when the
experience rudely exceeds ones level of understanding in an
out-of-control fashion.
