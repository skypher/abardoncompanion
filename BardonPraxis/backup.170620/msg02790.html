Hi guys:

I have a few questions concerning Step One of IIH. Hope to get some 
clarifications.

------------------------------------------

1. Since I have had some experience in meditation, when I do the TT, 
it's more like EOM because few thoughts come out. So,

Should I meditate naturally - meaning most often directly go to the 
EOM stage, or do I force it to stay in the TT stage.

Past Experience: 

1st TT exercise lasted for 5' - 10', a few thoughts and pictures 
aroused. 

Then a few failed attempts - falling to sleep before the relaxing 
procedures finished. Now I would always do the exercise in the sited 
position.

2nd TT exercise lasted for about 30'. Mind seemed to have shifted to 
another mode: this mode was still far away from Samadhi, still aware 
of the surroundings, but thoughts and images rarely aroused and if 
they do wouldn't last long, silent observer status is achieved. Maybe 
I can call this Meditative Mode I as compared to the pre-Meditative 
mode in which the mind is still try to calm down.

During the meditation, the outside of the brain is a little bit 
painful, similar to the situation when I listen to lectures in the 
dark and try to keep awake. I was not sleepy during the meditation 
though.

3rd TT exercise for about 30'. Almost no thoughts and images.

4th TT exercise got interesting: because too much noise in the 
house, I couldn't get into the Meditative Mode, instead, thoughts and 
images appeared many times. I even recalled two pieces of dreams that 
I dreamt the night before, tried to remember them after wake up and 
then forgot about this matter. 

So, it seems that I have found a way to experience TT, the question 
is should I "digress" from EOM to TT?
