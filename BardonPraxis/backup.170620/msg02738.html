Dear Karin,

>> 1) after the death of our physical body, will our mental body
re-integrate with the same Greater Self which generated it ? <<

Eventually, yes.

>> And an evolved mental body still be conscious of what it has learned
in previous incarnation ? <<

Yes, this knowledge is retained by the Individual Self or temporal
mental body. The "forgetfulness" occurs during the process of
incarnation.

>> 2) I feel my astral and mental bodies, but I cannot situate them
somewhere, I have some difficulties to seize the astral and mental
plane. Is the astral plan an energetic plan ? <<

It *feels* like what we experience physically as "energetic", but it is
actually composed of emotional significance.

>> And the mental one, is what ? I cannot perceive it. <<

The mental plane is consciousness or awareness.

I suggest the "Self-Healing Archaeous" Lessons Two and Three for a
better, experiential understanding of the astral and mental planes.

>> 3) In your answer to Eric you say that “all matter (mental, astral
and physical) is a consequence of the projection, and therefore
multiplication, of consciousness”. Is this skill of multiplication of
consciousness only due to Greater Self or un Individual Self could
multiply himself too? <<

An Individual Self is incapable of multiplication in the sense that the
Greater Self is.
