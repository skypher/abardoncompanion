Hello list Members,
I was previously on the list and have just come back after a long 
break. Just a quick history. I started learning Bardons teachings in 
the late 80s in a group of about 70+ people in Brisbane Australia.
We where taught quite quickly as we covered most of the three books 
in a short number of years. Obviously most people got left behind 
like myself and spent many years afterwards fixing the gaps in our 
training. I think our teacher new hew was only going to be here for a 
few years so he might as well transit as much as he can to those who 
can absorb it.
He did not mention Bardons books. It was not until 1990 that I came 
Bardons texts and put 2&2 together.
I live at the Gold Coast in Australia at present, but will be moving 
to Japan with my wife and daughter in July.
I teach martial arts and chi kung for a living, mainly private 
lessons. I plan on retiring completely from martial arts in July when 
I move to Japan.
I look forward to reading the many fine posts and occasionally 
contributing.
Blessings
Mark Rasmus
