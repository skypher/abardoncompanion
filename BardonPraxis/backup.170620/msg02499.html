Dear Peter,

While I haven't got the chance to read Questions & Answers yet, I am 
using that method myself. I don't know whether Bardon suggested the 
use of a spoken word or not, but I use a mental symbol in the same 
concept, and it worked very well.

Of course, the word (or any other personal symbol you choose) has no 
power of itself, but when repeatedly used during transition between 
mindsets (or even states of consciousness) it becomes associated 
with the change, and one of them brings the other along. The same 
logic stands behind using a special room for Magical work, special 
clothes, etc. The most important factor here is consistency, as 
changing the symbol every now and then will prevent the 
psychological binding from occuring.

After some time, or at least so it has been with me, the symbol 
becomes completely unnecessary, as you learn to discern the 
difference between the "special exercise mindset" and your regular 
mindset, and understand how to switch between them at will.. I find 
it difficult to express clearly in words.

Also, I personally believe it is important to do minor forms of the 
exercises throughout the whole day. By doing that, one can learn how 
to utilize his new tools all the time and in any situation, without 
necessarily going through the preparation procedure.

I hope I've been of help! :)
