> Dear Gordon,
> 
> >> Does how fast you perform your finger magick
> practices influence how
> fast you can develop it? I've been working on a fire
> element ritual for
> a couple weeks and this morning, while I was
> practicing it, I got hit
> with the question.
> If you increase the speed at which you perform the
> ritual, does that
> mean it's that much sooner before you are able to
> influence the mental,
> astral and physical realms or does the speed you
> perform at not matter
> that much? <<
> 
> The speed of your finger rituals will increase over
> time and with
> practice. At first though, it's best to NOT speed
> through them. Go
> slowly and make sure your finger ritual actually
> functions. It's the
> functionality of your finger ritual that determines
> it's effectiveness,
> not the speed with which you perform it. It's also
> the degree of
> functionality that determines the density of its
> effectiveness (e.g.,
> mental, astral or physical).
> 
> For example, with your Fire ritual. Work at its
> mental effectiveness
> first. Go slow and once you are certain it is
> effective on the mental
> level, build your speed. Then work on bringing it
> to an astral level of
> effectiveness. This might mean slowing down a bit
> at first but your
> speed with the astral ritual will increase with
> practice. Then when
> you've managed it at a mental and astral level, work
> at building its
> physical effectiveness.
> 
> The speed of these rituals is a function of your
> concentration. At
> first it takes a slow speed to maintain the proper
> degree of
> concentration. With practice, this degree of
> concentration comes faster
> and faster and your finger ritual speeds up *because
> it takes less time
> for you to reach the proper degree of
> concentration*.
