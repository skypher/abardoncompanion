I was curious about the connection between Bardon's system of 
Kaballah and general synesthesia. I have been reading many 
experiences on synesthesia and how colors are attached to many 
different concepts. I found a web page that gave a listing of of how 
two synesthetes saw the alphabet and the colors that they associated 
with them. The general experience was the same for both of them that 
they attributed different colors to different letters. The 
difference was how they associated different colors to different 
letters. I was wondering if the difference of perception/conception 
was because of the equilibrium being out of wack.

Now the another part

If the equilibrium were to be balanced and the letters that are in 
Bardon's Kaballah were looked at synesthetically, would the 
vibrations correspond, or are they arbitrary so as to make an 
internal system to better understand the external.

***I guess I am trying to get at the question if Bardon's system of 
Kaballah is arbitrary with some of the associations which become True 
and Factual over time, or if it is true to begin with.***

I am getting at two birds with one stone with Kaballah, and the 
nature of reality.
