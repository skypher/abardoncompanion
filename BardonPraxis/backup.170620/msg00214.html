Dear Martin ~

The following "trick" works for me, so perhaps you will find it 
useful.

When impregnating water via the Akasha, I create the ideation that 
the water contains the desired wish which I am about to drink. No 
news there, but following is how I make this conviction more real to 
me: I formulate the mindset I would have if I had just put some 
aspirin in the water against my headache. Instead of the ideation of 
headache-relief, however, I use whatever ideation I am pursuing. I 
build up the idea that I have put the "drug" that contains the 
essence of my wish into the glass of water, and then drink.

The same can be applied to the conscious intake of food and air, as 
well. In the case of food, I build up the idea that the food contains 
the nutrients that contain the essence of my wish. In the case of 
air, I build up the idea that the air is saturated with a gas which 
contains the essence of my wish.

Perhaps this will help. In the case of magnetic/electric 
impregnation, I would instead directly accumulate the ideation into 
the medium, but I'm not at that stage of development yet. Akashic 
impregnation is purely mental. Still, perhaps this will help you see 
the difference between the two forms of impregnation (impregnation of 
the fluids vs impregnation of the akasha).

Well met
Vovin
