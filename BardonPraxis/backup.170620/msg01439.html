Since so called "inner alchemy" is a purely psychological practice, the
laboratory work of practical Alchemy is not a necessary aspect of its
pursuit. However, if you're truly interested in pursuing Alchemy,

suggest that you read, read, read and meditate, meditate, meditate on as
many Alchemical tomes as you can get your hands on until it is feasible
for you to set up a laboratory.
