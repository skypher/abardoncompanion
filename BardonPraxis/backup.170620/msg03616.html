to such "feeling good", Dave.

winds coming from a dry environment down a mountain, you often develop
air which is charged wrongly --- such as the Santa Anna winds in
California. In those environments, folks don't feel right, are very
edgy, and bad luck and destructive accidents seem to increase ....
