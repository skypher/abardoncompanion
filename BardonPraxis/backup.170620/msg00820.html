The planetary spheres represent differing degrees of density, not
'locations' (in the physical sense of the word). In other words,
*every* physical place is surrounded by an astral "zone girdling the
earth", which in turn is surrounded by a "moon zone", then a "mercury
zone", and so on.

The planetary spheres really have nothing to do with the physical
planets. For example, if you were to travel physically to the moon, you
would not be traveling to closer proximity of the "moon zone".

Only the "zone girdling the earth" exists within the astral realm and is
therefore reachable with the astral body. The "moon zone" outward all
exist within the mental realm and are therefore not reachable with the
astral body. However, within the "zone girdling the earth" all the
outer planetary zones are reflected and these reflections can be reached
with the astral body.
