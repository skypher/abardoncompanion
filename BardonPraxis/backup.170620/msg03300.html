I'm certain that once you've spent time with this process you will 
realize what number of items listed are right for yourself. You will 
notice later, in Step VII I think that again, you are admonished to 
work again on the elements within your spirit body, to correct 
unstable conditions that present themselves. You will find in fact 
that in many steps the process is mentioned in such as way as to 
suggest that you may not be capable or able to advance and have good 
results if you are not 'balanced' with magical and elemental 
equilibrium, and the process presents itself in different ways of 
using the elements to balance the self. 

Even though it may seem that I am suggesting that it would not 
benefit you to look and work on/for 100 examples I am in fact not 
doing so. It all depends of course on how much prior work on the 
personality elements within oneself a person has done using other 
methods perhaps. Once you've identified a few solid examples though, 
be encouraged to 'address' the issue presented and to eradicate it 
from your personality. You will 'always' be working for the rest of 
your journey on eradicating and balancing your personality and 
character as you find more and more how Divine Providence virtures 
are or are not a degree of your own characer.


In fact I was amused and mildly shocked to find that almost all of 
the other 9 steps of the 10 step Bardon technique is covered in the 
first step. Just different shades of deeper and wider landscapes of 
the same presented material.
