You know as hermetics student, or just people on Earth - :)-, we are 
very often (if not always) experiencing situations in our life which 
are the reflection of our inner issues. 

In your exemple, from the outside life, you have the following image :

"and meditations are 
> interrupted by children with paintscrapers charging from room to 
> room"

And from the inside, in fact "your children are your thought". This 
means that possibly you have difficulties in your medidations because 
they are interupted by impromptus though which pops up like "children 
with ...". 

As every psychotherapist knows and have experienced, if changing from 
inside, outside life situation are also changing. FOr example, if I 
stop worried for my job because of fearing to be fired, I will not 
always experience situation where jobs is painfull, stressfull and 
where I am obliged to work 70hours/week with no time to meditate.
But also, which is seldom known and used, the association can be 
reversed : you can work from outside situations to improve your inner 
life. And this is much more powerfull than the former one.
If taking your exemple, just explaining your children that you 
meditate or relax, and some quietness is needed for a while will help 
by itself your meditation practise. This way, you will also educate 
your thought (not only your children, you can also start with the 
blessing of food with them, good substitute for food impregnation). 
In my exemple, this will be giving up both the job and the fear.

This lead to a powerfull technics to help personal development :
- Examine how a life situation is merely reflecting an inner issue.
- Resonate with the association (it means FEEL IT FROM THE INSIDE, 
understanding is not enough) 
- Work on this life situation with the goal to help the inner issue & 
Resonate (FEEL) the work. 

This is a possible way to put the life this real meaning, to me, the 
reflection of our inner state. 

In fact this is simple daily magic ... with no ritual. Eveyday life 
possible magic. 

best wishes for success,
