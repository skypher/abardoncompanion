RM and I were discussing how when we focus, say, on the Bardon mental 
exercises (or spirituality in general), other habits, such as formal 
exercise, seem to suffer. It seemed to me that applying elemental 
equilibrium to Life is a point that Bardon makes but skims over (or perhaps 
we are skimming over it <shrug>).

I have noticed that during a typical day, I seem to draw on a finite (and 
diminishing) reservoir of Will. It's as if when you use Will something gets 
consumed and has to be replenished over time. Practice certainly seems to 
strengthen Will but only to a point (in my experience). Is there a Hermetic way 
to increase the size of the reservoir of Will that a person can draw upon? 

Sincerely, /RM/
