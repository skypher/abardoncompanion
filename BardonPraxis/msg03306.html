different idea about the soul mirror that works very well for me, maybe it will 
for you: instead of failings, think of mistakes. Actually record not something 
general but the very *moment* when you realised your behaviour was not what 
you might have hoped. Like a moment in a story. This happened then I did this. 
Then when correcting the behaviour it is like rewriting the story. Will work 
well if you are a big reader especially. Jason
