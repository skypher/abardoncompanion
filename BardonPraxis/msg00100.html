> and Eucharist Magick focused on increasing my social circle. 
> Throughout that month nothing of note happened.(!) :(

> I eventually gave up and switched my efforts to CIB and EM

This is a different viewpoint, think of it what you will. :-):

By focusing, you positioned yourself on the other side of your 
goal, that is actually "small social circle" (or what it meant 
for you). As you gave up, you consciously accepted that condition. 
You decided that reality is actually "small social circle".

And so it manifested. 

It manifested because it had to, because both poles are true. 

"Everything is dual; everything has poles; everything has its pair of
opposites; like and unlike are the same; opposites are identical in
nature, but different in degree; extremes meet; all truths are but
half-truths; all paradoxes may be reconciled."--The Kybalion.

As you focus, take care that you do not place yourself in the 
position of not having achieved the goal, or just pushing it 
into the future. Preferrably, perform as if you have already 
achieved the goal. ...but if that is too difficult, perform without 
expectation.

I think that the soul mirror comes in handy here, because it makes
us conscious of who we think we are, and likewise it is a polar 
mirror of the world. I think it helps us see why we desire that 
which we do--why we perceive reality the way we do.
