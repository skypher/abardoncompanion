Dear Peter,

>> Then, of course, the question becomes: does the initiate who has
completed step eight really need such training wheels? <<

Most likely, yes. There is a LOT to keep track of in a full blown
evocation ritual and even after completing Step Eight, this takes some
time to get used to. Having the magical regalia helps immensely in this
process.

>> Also, why does Bardon not talk about the invocation of a spirit into
the structure of the mental body? <<

There's no profit, magically speaking, to be had in letting another
spirit occupy or fill your mental body. However, there is great profit
in the process of *merging with* another spirit, such as in the Step
Four transference of consciousness and the Step Ten work of merging with
the Divine.
