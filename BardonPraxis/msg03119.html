There's an interesting study quoted in Mona Lisa Schultz's "Awakening 
Intuition", which is about clairvoyant/medical intuition. Multiple 
personality patients were studied and found to have different vision 
abilities based on the "personality" that was active. It follows that 
vision deficiencies are not absolutely physical in nature. FWIW, 
after I began meditation and spiritual work, my contact lense 
prescription was reduced by half...and I am 44.

Interesting stuff.
