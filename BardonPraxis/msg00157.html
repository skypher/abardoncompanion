Dear Newbie,

>> a) I compared The Occult Anatomy Of the Body p. 38 (particularly its

/meditate on further, or was the Picture just an illustration? <<

It's just rough illustration of some of the more important polarities.

>> b) F. Bardon also mentions the tarot p. 26, but with another

Yes, or at least that genre.

>> c) I am re-reading the theory, and I have the temptation of making
the relationship with the Kybalion at the same time, to find common
tracks. Will it lead to something, or is it a waste of time at my level
? <<

meditation. :)
