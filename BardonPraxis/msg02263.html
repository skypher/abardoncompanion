Good morning David:

Eventually yes, the practice of Hermetics leads to strong, well-
balanced energy bodies to which many people will feel an attraction. 
Sometimes however the early stages of the exercises create temporary 
problems with the aura. Soul mirror work can call up powerful 
unresolved issues that influence the aura. In the early stages, pore 
breathing can also cause the kinds of problems that were mentioned in 
Allen's e-mail. In the long term, pore breathing develops the energy 
body and expands and strengthens the energy pathways within. In the 
early stages however little pockets of energy accumulation can occur 
and these discharge at random moments. It's harmless but it can make 
the people around you edgy.
