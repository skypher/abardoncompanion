I'd say that the sensations in your head are all related to eye tension
and probably to "knitting" your brow (pinching the brows together).

anything to do with emptiness or with visualization.

Commentary Upon IIH and, in the "Essays and Correspondence" section,
you'll find several excerpts from past correspondence that relate
directly to these exercises. I think some of them might be of help to
you overcoming these difficulties.
