I agree. I don't see competition as way of proving myself better than
others. I see it as an opportunity to test myself. Take basketball for
example...you could be an excellent shooter when you've got the court to
yourself and there are no distractions (a passive environment), but what
happens when there is a defender there, doing his best to stop you
scoring? (an active environment). Things you'd have been able to do
before with minimal effort/focus now require complete concentration. And
with this state of mind, competition stops being about winning, and
starts to become a dance between you and your defender.
