Dear group,

Within The Practice of Magical Evocation Bardon talks about how upon 
the death of a magician his ritual tools should be destroyed so that 
they will not be improperly used by another person. Yet with the 
magician that has gone through the curriculum of IIH and KTQ their 
ritual tools is their own body, in particular their own organs which 
they have acclimitated and transformed to various sorts of energies. 
Namely, vital energy, the elements, the fluids and the letters. 
Something that fundementally alters the structure of the organs at a 
very deep level. Now, my question is: should the magician allow his 
organs to be transplanted into another person's body upon his death? 
And, if so, what would be the possible side effects for the person 
that has a magicians' organs transplanted into his own body?
