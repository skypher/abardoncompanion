On p54 of ?Frobato the Magician? Franz Bardon was demonstrating to the 
professor that man?s experience does not end with what we call death; on the 
contrary, true life begins.
After the experiment, the professor explains his outer body experiences, that 
he was able to witness what Frabato and his assistants did to his body. 
An individual without any training in magic will, after his death, be unable to 
perceive any sensory impression from the physical world.
The difference between this experience and death is the sudden severing of the 
astral matrix.

the sensory impressions after his actual death? If for example he was 
resurrected after few days, will he be able describe what happened in the 
physical world during those few days like the professor was describing?

dissolves back in the universe?
