Dear Uthlagus,

>> Sorry to be a pesky newbie (one of the oldest on record), but what
is/was Solomon's Temple which Bardon refers to in my favourite quote,
and which I bear in mind constantly. <<

This is Solomon's Temple from the Old Testament / Torah. However,
Bardon used the term metaphorically, in good occultist fashion, to
symbolize the perfected human or adept. This is a common symbol in
Western occultism, especially Freemasonry. The labor of initiation is
said to be the building of one's own "Temple of Solomon".

>> Also, what on earth is goetia? <<

This is part of the famous "Key of Solomon", known also as "Clavicula
Salomonis Regis" or "Lemegeton". The "Goetia" is the first of five
parts in this work.
