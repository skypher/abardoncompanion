Hi,

I tried the merkabah for several months, and I don't
think the two systems can be compared. Drun just
gives a tool to increase your energy, whereas Bardon
lays out the structure to integrate ascension into
every part of your life. Energetically, the merkabah
feels mostly electrical, but that may just be my
predilection. I guess both systems deal with raising
your vibration, but isn't that the basic premis of
every form of initiaion? I could never seem to go
forward with the Merkabah when I got to a certain
point when things got a bit "charged up." Merkabah
definitely brings up soul mirror issues, as your
energy is amplified. The kundalini itself is supposed
to burn through those issues so that you can become
aware of the lightbody, and increase your
consciousness. That's all great, my only problem is
what to do with my spare time when I am done
meditating. There's no integration; I still have my
dreams but the increase in energy doesn't really help
me to work more intelligently or creatively. It helps
to stimulate the mental faculty, but in my case I was
already too mental, so it exacerbated the air element.
It could have been brought back into balance if I
continued with the correct meditative attitude. The
soul mirror will help with any other system. I spent
a lot of time trying to figure out how the two systems
were compatible, but now that is just another item on
my soul mirror. I will always be intrigued about the
fantastic technique, but the only way I can concieve
of proving its reality is to master IIH. 

namaste
Dustin
