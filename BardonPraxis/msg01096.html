Dear Nenad,

>> If this is a rudimentary clairvoiance, it is my opinion that this is
happening in the very early stage of practice. <<

True clairvoyance is more than the ability to perceive the subtle
physical energy that surrounds all living matter. With true
clairvoyance one simultaneously understands what one is perceiving. In
other words, *information* is a part of clairvoyant perception. This is
because clairvoyance is the perception of astral and mental energy.
What you have described is the perception of subtle *physical* energies
that are not normally perceived by the human eye.

>> It is my understanding that this sort of ability comes from
practising visualization and it is analog to the Will, and dependent on
the level of magical equilibrium. <<

What you say is accurate so far as true clairvoyance is concerned, but
inaccurate in relation to the perception of subtle physical energies.

>> I am very suspicious when things fall easy in my lap. Did anyone had
any similar experiences this early in the work? Don`t get me wrong,
it`s a wondefull feeling :-) <<

Well, don't let your suspicion inhibit your enjoyment! Some gifts
deserve to be savored for what they are, without questioning them. :)

I suggest that you begin trying to also perceive what each color means,
what each color communicates to you.
