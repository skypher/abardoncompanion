>> I was wondering if you could elaborate further on what the "dark
night of the
soul" is or maybe at least why it is experienced. <<

The term "dark night of the soul" is used to describe the period after
one has crossed the Abyss and has experienced the eternal perspective.
This experience is very disorienting to the personal, temporal layers of
Self. Symptoms at the personal level are feelings of "why bother?",
"what's the point?", etc. The initiate must reconcile the eternal and
temporal perspectives and arrive at reasons for continuing their
temporal existence.
