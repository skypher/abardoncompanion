>> 24 steps in High Magic by Franz Bardon

This was not *written* by Bardon. What it is, is "Seven Hermetic
Letters" by Georg Lomer, which Bardon had translated into Czech for his
students. The translated manuscript was later discovered among Bardon's
papers and translated again into German and then English. It was
translated so many times that it wasn't recognized as being Lomer's book
until much later.

A copy of the many-times-translated version of "High Magic" can be found
as an appendix to the English (Ruggeberg) edition of "Frabato".

The edition advertised quite frequently on EBay is a rip-off.
