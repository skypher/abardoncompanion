Happy birthday BardonPraxis!

With great coincidence this anniversary falls exactly on my own 17th 
birthday, and I would like to take advantage of this splendid 
opportunity to thank all of you here on this group.
During the past 6 months that I have frequented this message board, 
I gained innumerable insights and bits of understanding about the 
questions and issues which were bothering me regarding the Hermetic 
Work, even though that I myself participated only once or twice in 
the discussions.
Thank you all dear friends for your indirect support, especially to 
you Rawn with your articles and wise replies, as I doubt I would 
have been able to muster enough perseverance to go on with the Work 
without the existence of this group.

I sincerely believe that this group is of genuine importance to all 
students of Bardon who came across it on their path, and I wish it 
and all of its inhabitants nothing but the best. May it be blessed 
with a constant inflow of high-quality messages, and will continue 
to bestow its benefits on all those who visit it - to whom I wish 
the best progress on their individual path.
Of course, thank you Rawn, for creating and maintaining this group, 
and doing an excellent job moderating it :)

Happy birthday!

Daniel
