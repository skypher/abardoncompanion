>> A few words to relate what happened to me during the mental emptiness
exercise. I was performing also this exercice as usual, in order tokeep
on involving my quality of emptiness (allthough Ireached Level 2). I was
centered as usual (about the crane), when suddenly, it happend that my
"gravity center" migrated to a zone between my cheast and the solar
plexus. As a matter of fact, I felt a peace and a calm much more
impressive and important than it used to be. I had to take care, because

English edition). You can read about this in the introduction to Step
Five.
