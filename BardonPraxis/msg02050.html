Dear (Sir)

Thanks for your note. _Paths of Wisdom_ has been out of print for just
over a year now, and I know a lot of people have been trying to get
copies. I wish I had a private stash to sell, but no such luck. The
rights will revert to me in February of 2004. I've already discussed a
reprint with another publisher, and once I get the rights back it 
should be available pretty quickly, but there's that unavoidable 
delay!

I plan on expanding the book a little, putting in material on aspects 
of Cabalistic theory and practice that were over my head in 1994-5 
(when the original was written). So I hope it will be worth the wait.

Best,

John Michael Greer
