At last!  The new Expanded Second Edition of A Bardon Companion is in print!

This second edition contains an entirely new in-depth commentary upon Initiation Into Hermetics and a large collection of excerpts from my public and private correspondence.  I've also reproduced the complete text of the original 2002 first edition and have supplied a full index.  In total, the expanded second edition of 2010 is 520 pages in length -- over twice the material presented in the first edition!  It is a hefty book, weighing in at 2.8 lbs and with a size of 6"x9"x1.2"!  LOL

Since my usual publisher was unable to afford such an expensive print run, I have opted to publish this second edition myself, using an internet based on-demand publisher (CreateSpace.com).  Since CreateSpace is located in the US, all orders will be shipped (by CreateSpace) from the US -- which unfortunately means that non-US readers will incur more expensive shipping charges (determined by CreateSpace) and longer shipping times.
