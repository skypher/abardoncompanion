New!!!  A Bardon Companion @ SecondLife  New!!!

A few months ago I purchased a small plot of virtual-land in SecondLife and created the Bardon Companion Homeland as an online interface with Bardonists worldwide.  Each Sunday, I host a live discussion group at the ABC Homeland in SecondLife.  These wide ranging, though Bardon-related, discussions begin at 9:00 pm Central European Time (CET), which equals 8:00 pm GMT / 12noon PST, in order to accommodate the broadest range of locations and time zones.  Discussion lasts for about 90 minutes.  To attend, you will need a high-speed internet connection and a computer with speakers (microphone encouraged).  Attendance also necessitates setting up a free account with SecondLife and creating an avatar.

Fee is L$2000 (an internal SecondLife currency called Linden$) or €7.50 (pay via PayPal to rawn@rawnmade.com).  No one turned away for lack of funds!  I hope to see you there! :)
