Links to Bardon Resources on the WWW


BOOKS:

Languages of Bardon: An alphabetical listing of the various languages into which Bardon's books have been translated, as well as their publishers and availability.

Faulks Books:  This is the British distributor for Merkur Books and for my own books.

Josuah L. Hutchinson Publishing:  Josuah publishes my own writings, "A Bardon Companion" and "Eight Temples Meditation Project".<

Moryason.com:  Alexandre Moryason is the translator and publisher of Bardon's books into French.

Rüggeberg Publishing:  Dieter Rüggeberg has been publishing Bardon's work since the late 1950's and is the main person responsible for keeping Bardon's work available to the general public.



DISCUSSION  BOARDS:

FranzBardonMagi:  This is an unmoderated, English language discussion board on Yahoo Groups.  Over 1000 members worldwide. 

E_Franzbardon:  This is another unmoderated English language discussion board on Yahoo Groups with just over 300 members.  Unfortunately, the owner has pretty much abandoned it and there is frequent spam of an unsavory nature.

FRANZ_BARDON:  This is an unmoderated French language discussion board on Yahoo Groups.  This board seems very active!

Frabato.com:  This is a French language website with lots of information and a discussion board for registered members.  Replaces the old MSN Frabato discussion group.

Forum 63373:  This is a very active German language discussion board.

Franzbardon:  This is a Portuguese language discussion board on Yahoo Groups with just over 75 members and a surprising amount of activity.

Amigos de Franz Bardon:  This is a Spanish language discussion board on Yahoo Groups.  Very small membership so far.


INFORMATION:

BardonPraxis Discussion Group Message Archive:  The complete, searchable archive of messages from the BardonPraxis yahoogroup which was active from December 2002 through April 2005.

Frabato.com:  This is a French language website with lots of information and a discussion board for registered members.

FranzBardon.pl:  THE Polish language Bardon-related website with many translations of my own work!

Hermetics.com:  A very helpful (updated) links page of sites related to Bardon.

Hermetics.org:  This is an eclectic site, filled with a variety of  information.  It has a wonderful library of links to all sorts of sites relevant to Hermetics, Alchemy, Kabbala, etc.  It can be viewed in either Turkish or English. 

TMO-WG.net:  Internet home for "The Magic of IHVH-ADNI" (a.k.a. TMO).


ORGANIZATIONS:

TMO Working Group:  Internet Portal for "The Magic of IHVH-ADNI" (a.k.a. TMO) and for the TMO Working Group.  The Working Group is dedicated to actively exploring the applications of TMO in the group context.   A great resource for TMO practitioners and gateway for the TMO-WG Academy which supports those learning TMO from the audio Lessons.  Available in English, French and Polish.


RAWN'S  NON-BARDON  FAVORITES:

DemocracyNow!:  This is the only reliable non-corporate one-hour daily news program in the USA.  If you live in the US and want to find out what's really happening in your country and in the rest of the world, or if you live outside of the US and want to know what's really going on there, then I HIGHLY recommend this daily news hour presented by Amy Goodman and Juan Gonzalez!  You can read a text version of the program, watch a streaming video or listen to streaming audio.  All past programs are also archived in all three formats.

Damanhur:  A very interesting community / "nation" located in Italy, founded upon very progressive social, ecological and spiritual principles.  A beautiful website to visit!  :)

BBC 3 Radio:  Radio reception is very poor and the choices are few where I live so I listen to radio online and have found BBC 3 Radio to offer the best variety of "World Music".  My favorite program is Andy Kershaw's weekly show!  :)

Andy Foulds:  This is an amazing website built by a graphic designer. Try out the very fun amusements and check out the innovative samples.

CD Baby:  When it comes to buying obscure CDs by equally obscure musicians, this is THE place to go.  Not only is their selection and variety great but they provide THE best, most humorous shopping experience I've ever encountered online!  Just receiving their shipping confirmation notice was worth more than the cost of the CDs I ordered!  I was laughing for days!  ;-)
